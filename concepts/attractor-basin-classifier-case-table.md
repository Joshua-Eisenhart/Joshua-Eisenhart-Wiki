## Reading rule
`deep_basin` is not a universal success label. It is a local label for the named receipt claim under this classifier.

The important evidence is the spread:
- some rows are anti-basin;
- some remain candidate;
- some remain open boundaries;
- only a few count as deep basin under the local criteria.

That spread is the anti-teleology pattern: many possible futures/trajectories are kept visible, and no single basin story is privileged by default.
