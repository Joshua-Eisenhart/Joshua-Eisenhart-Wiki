# Research Inventory and Foundational Findings

## Overview
Evidence-backed inventory of the actual research results simulated so far. Not a speculative wish list. The system is converging on a small set of primitive building blocks: density matrices, admissible probe families, Pauli/Clifford operator bases, partial trace and CPTP structure, correlation tensors, and admissible geometry.

## Key Findings by Probe

**State representations at L0:** 9/9 survive. Purification ranked highest. Coherence vector best d=4-extensible. Density matrix canonical. Eigenvalues weak (lose phase). Husimi/Wigner lossy.

**Geometry families at L0:** Trace distance, Bures, Fubini-Study, QFI, QGT survived. HS-alone too flat. Relative entropy not a metric. Real-only kills Berry/curvature. Commutative kills geometry. The admissible geometry is curved, complex, and probe-relative.

**Entropy battery:** Spectral families agree on qubit mixedness ordering. Diagonal Shannon is basis-dependent. Product-proxy conditional entropy adds no signal. No simple spectral entropy rescues pure-state Fi blind spot.

**Correlation/Axis 0 evidence:** Local-only L|R families fail. Xi_shell is strongest candidate bridge family. Coherent information is the signed cut quantity that matters. Total correlation alone is not enough.

## Five Irreducible Primitives

1. density matrices
2. probes
3. operator algebra (Pauli/Clifford/noncommutation)
4. partial traces and channel structure
5. admissible geometry on the same carrier

Everything else should be tested as a derived view or candidate compression of these primitives.

## Practical Consequences

Next simulations should ask: what does this family reduce to under admissible probes? What geometry does it preserve or destroy? What does it detect that simpler families miss? What is the correct collapse class? What survives the negative controls?
