Source digest for the mass equivalence engine. Deeper treatment of structural equivalence classes and operational meaning.

Based on current research, the most robust primitives are: density matrices, admissible probes, partial trace/channel structure, noncommuting operator algebra, curved information geometry. Entropy is important but not first.
