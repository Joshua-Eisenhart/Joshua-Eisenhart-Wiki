## How This Relates to Current Work

Current active work is the base constraint/entropy/geometry stack — not the macro manifold itself. The manifold framing matters now because current work is also a language-control problem: one vocabulary for lower-layer sim stack, another for higher collective/mimetic layer, must not flatten them together.

**Practical implication:** When running current bounded sweeps, doc language should stay disciplined. Candidate measures remain candidates until they survive the relevant layer. Entropy is not allowed to pretend it is primitive. Geometry is not allowed before carrier and probe relations are admitted. Collective manifold vocabulary stays separated from lower-layer evidence stack.
