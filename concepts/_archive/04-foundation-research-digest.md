# Foundation Research Digest

## Overview
Source digest for foundation research. Covers the core mathematical foundations that underpin the system.

## Key Foundational Structures
- Density matrices as base objects (not distributions)
- Admissible probe families (what tests exist)
- CPTP maps / channels (admissible transformations)
- Partial trace (bipartite -> marginal)
- Operator algebra (Pauli, Clifford generators)
- Constraint on distinguishability as primitive

## What's NOT Foundation
- Entropy is NOT foundational (it is a later admissible measure)
- Axes are NOT foundational (they are families of constrained operations)
- Specific geometry is NOT foundational (it is what the constraints admit)
