# Axis 0 Spec Options

## Overview
This page preserves the older Axis-0 proposal family: correlation persistence polarity under perturbation, with several possible operationalizations.

Authority boundary: raw docs here are genealogy/spec-option sources, not proof of current Axis-0 admission.

## Main points
- Axis 0 is framed as allostatic vs homeostatic response under perturbation.
- The proposal families include pairwise mutual-information spread, variance control, total correlation, and path-ensemble history variety.
- Option-space here also overlaps the later `j/k`-fuzz and `i`-scalar cluster: path-ensemble history variety is one of the public entry points into that family.
- The document is explicitly option-space, not canon.
- The operational question is how to measure correlation spread or damping in a finite admissible system.
