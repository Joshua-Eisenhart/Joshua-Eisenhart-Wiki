## Key Corrections

WRONG: Tiers are floors in a building. RIGHT: M(C) is a simultaneous surface. All constraints coexist. Tiers are resolution levels for finite agent exploration, not ontological layers. The geometry chain (S^3 -> Hopf -> Weyl -> engines) is a COORDINATE CHART on M(C), not a construction sequence.

### 2. The process is self-grounding
F01+N01 are not external axioms applied to a substrate. They are the substrate. The constraints don't "act on" something -- they ARE the structure. See [[constraint-surface-and-process]].

### 3. Downward-blind information flow
Layer N sees 0..N-1, blind to N+1+. This is motivated by N01 + anti-salience, not "derived from" N01 in the strict sense.

### 4. Multiple boots simultaneously
A0/A1/A2/B/SIM run simultaneously with contamination rules. The boots must remain separate. See [[agent-workflow-and-boot-architecture]].

### 5. Harness construction is the meta-goal
a=a iff a~b is harness infrastructure, not just a philosophical statement. The current work is building the agent vocabulary that constrains how agents think.

### 6. QIT engines ARE the universal pattern
Not a model of something else. Under every probe applied across scales, the engine's correlation structure has not been distinguished from patterns found at other scales. This is a nominalist claim, not a Platonic one.
