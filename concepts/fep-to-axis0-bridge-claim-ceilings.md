# FEP to Axis0 Bridge Claim Ceilings

Axis0 is the open bridge spine. FEP does not close it.

## Safe relation

FEP can provide support language for:

- boundary/blanket questions;
- prediction-first update loops;
- persistence under uncertainty;
- information-geometry neighbor terms;
- active search and epistemic probe value.

FEP cannot provide direct evidence for:

- `Xi_fuzz`;
- `rho_AB`;
- `Phi0`;
- Axis0 cut kernels;
- gravity;
- field-wide compression;
- consciousness.

## Bridge ceiling table

| Claim | Maximum FEP support | Stronger evidence required |
|---|---|---|
| Boundary matters | external support | local cut/partition/readout artifact |
| Prediction-first is relevant | external support | implemented model/update path |
| Markov blanket suggests cut language | analogy | finite cut carrier and conditional/blocked-readout evidence |
| Information geometry may help | support | explicit metric/divergence/invariant over carrier |
| Axis0 bridge is closed | none | dedicated Axis0 evidence packet |
| `Phi0` is solved | none | dedicated bridge result and admission gate |
| Gravity is solved | none | physics-specific derivation and independent checks |

## Banned sentences

```text
FEP proves Axis0.
The Markov blanket is the Axis0 cut.
Expected free energy is Phi0.
Particular physics solves gravity.
Active inference implements Leviathan's world engine.
```

## Safe prompt for future work

```text
Use FEP only to propose finite boundary/probe fixtures for Axis0-adjacent questions.
For each fixture, name the carrier, partition, readout, update, negative case,
and the exact claim ceiling. Do not promote bridge closure without a local result.
```
