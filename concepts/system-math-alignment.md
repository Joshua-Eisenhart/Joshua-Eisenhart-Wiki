## Overview
System math alignment identifies which mathematical families are already native to the stack and how compression, geometry, and operator structure fit together.

## Main points
- The system already speaks spectral language.
- Compression belongs where it can be stated as principal-subspace retention or low-rank operator approximation.
- Geometry should remain carrier-compatible rather than being imported as an abstract overlay.
- The useful phrasing is specific: covariance spectral compression, Schmidt-mode truncation, density-operator low-rank approximation, principal-subspace retention.
