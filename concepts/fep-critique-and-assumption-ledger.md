# FEP Critique and Assumption Ledger

This page is the overclaim brake for the FEP atlas.

## Critique anchors

| Source | Main pressure | Wiki consequence |
|---|---|---|
| Biehl, Pollock, Kanai | blanket definitions are not all equivalent; some steps in original free-energy arguments require extra assumptions | name which blanket/derivation sense is being used |
| Aguilera, Millidge, Tschantz, Buckley | Markov-blanket and solenoidal-flow requirements may hold only in restricted parameter spaces; some inference links require strong assumptions | do not generalize FEP to all local systems without proof |

## Assumption checklist

Before any page makes a strong FEP-derived claim, ask:

- What is the state space?
- What is the partition?
- What conditional independence is asserted?
- Is this statistical, dynamical, physical, or analogy-only?
- What steady-state or ergodicity condition is assumed?
- Is solenoidal flow being invoked?
- What density is variational and what density is claimed as target?
- What local evidence would falsify the mapping?

## Overclaim patterns

| Pattern | Correction |
|---|---|
| "Every persistent thing minimizes free energy, so this system is FEP." | Say only that FEP offers a support vocabulary unless local assumptions are checked. |
| "There is a boundary, so there is a Markov blanket." | Boundary and conditional-independence blanket are not identical. |
| "The blanket is the Axis0 cut." | Axis0 requires local bridge evidence. |
| "Prediction-first implies consciousness." | Prediction-first supports perception/memory modeling only. |
| "Particular physics solves the physics model." | Particular physics is external support, not local closure. |

## Safe use

- Attach critique references to every broad FEP page.
- Use this ledger to demote claims to analogy/support when assumptions are missing.
- Prefer local finite fixtures when possible.
