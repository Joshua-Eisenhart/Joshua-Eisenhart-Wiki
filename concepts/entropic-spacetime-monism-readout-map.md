## The Nominalist Form

The safe claim is not that all labels are magically identical.

The safe claim is:

```text
same underlying substrate, different probe/regime/readout
```

In nominalist terms, physics names are cuts made by probes, scales, constraints, and persistence tests. The model says not to reify the cuts as separate substances.

So `gravity`, `space`, `time`, `dark energy`, `dark matter`, `matter`, `mass`, `energy`, `baryon`, `hadron`, and `force` are not primitive names. They are candidate stable distinctions in one substrate, under some probe family `M`.

Root discipline:

```text

```

A “thing” is a stable distinction only when the active probes can separate it and the support can preserve it. Matter is where spacetime becomes locally distinguishable as a bounded object. The flat whole-field view may see the same structure as one smooth spacetime-information field.

## Gravity Recoupled To Entropic Monism

The corrected gravity reading is:

```text
gravity = local synchronization pressure between bounded spacetime knots
          and the global spacetime / information / possibility field
```

Under this reading:

1. **The base field is flat/smooth from the whole-field perspective.** Matter and dark matter may flatten into one spacetime-information field from that view.
2. **Matter is not in spacetime like marbles in a box.** Matter is bounded spacetime: a knot, bubble, fold, or stabilized local configuration.
3. **Dark matter is not a separate primitive stuff.** It is preserved information / negative entropy / prematter-like spacetime, possibly loop-like or lower-dimensional in the source language.
4. **Dark energy is the same substrate in the global expansion regime.** It is positive-entropy expansion / possibility growth.
5. **Gravity is the same substrate in a local constrained regime.** A bounded knot “sees” the field as non-flat and experiences the synchronization as weight, pull, curvature, or force.
6. **Entanglement is the connective condition.** Without correlation, the field is not one updating object; with correlation, local knots can be synchronized against the whole field.

So the deepest story is not:

```text
mass sits inside spacetime and pulls other mass
```

The owner-model story is closer to:

```text
spacetime/entropy forms bounded knots;
knots locally see a non-flat readout;
the whole field synchronizes correlations;
that local synchronization readout is gravity.
```
