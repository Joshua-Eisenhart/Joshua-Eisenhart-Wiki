# Mimetic Meme Manifold

## Overview
The mimetic meme manifold is the macro language-coupling layer: a salience-shaping vocabulary system for loading a model or group into the right conceptual space.

## What it does
- Shapes what becomes salient in an LLM
- Supports boot and harness design
- Couples group language, membership, and narrative
- Provides project-specific dictionaries on top of a base vocabulary

## What it is not
- Not the starting point for proving the lower-layer math
- Not a substitute for admissibility tests
- Not a claim that language is the primitive substrate
