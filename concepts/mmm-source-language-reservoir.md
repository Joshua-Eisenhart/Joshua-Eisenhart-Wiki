# MMM Source Language Reservoir

## Purpose
This page is a source-language reservoir for MMM / mini-MMM harness work. It collects aligned terms, phrases, sentence patterns, and drift rewrites from the READ ONLY source corpora so future LLM prompts can be pre-loaded with the system's saliency surface.

Important correction: MMMs are not rules. They are a language sea: source quotes, project terms, research-field nouns, couplings, triples, and sentence rhythms that bias future completions toward the project's kind of thinking. They may carry exact quotes, but quote force is saliency force, not authority force.

This is not a canon page by itself. It is a language reservoir: lower-entropy material can carry stronger kernel saliency; high-entropy material is fuel/overlay and must not be cited as authority unless later admitted by repo/wiki evidence gates. For the richer quote/coupling/triple layer, see [[mmm-language-sea-quotes-couplings-triples]].

## 2026-06-05 saliency delta

The current MMM gap is not only vocabulary volume. Future LLMs need saliency pressure that resists three default collapses:

1. **Canon collapse** — treating the wiki as a proof surface instead of a routed field of likely candidates, support lanes, source kernels, result ledgers, and open possibilities.
2. **Identity collapse** — merging geometry-ratchet, Axis0 shell, Holodeck process, and cross-field genealogy because they share loop shape.
3. **Research-normalization collapse** — translating the model into familiar causal/teleological/ontology-first research language and losing the inversion that made the model interesting.

Useful injection sentences:

- `The wiki is not canon; it is an aligned field of candidates, source kernels, evidence routes, and research pressure lenses.`
- `A support source can improve saliency without changing admission status.`
- `Shared loop shape is not identity proof; keep the readouts separate until a finite bridge survives controls.`
- `Future-option pressure is not teleology: many viable continuations constrain the present without one future being privileged.`
- `Geometry/algebra receipts can strengthen the carrier layer while leaving the Axis0/gravity bridge open.`
- `Nominalist language names the probe, support, quotient, and killed alternatives before it names an object.`

Pressure lenses to add into later MMM packets: viability theory, basin stability, cybernetics, autopoiesis/enactivism, active inference, path-integral control, quality diversity/novelty search, operational probabilistic theories, QIT geometry, Hopf/spinor/quaternion/Clifford support, and LLM ontology-smuggling failure modes.
