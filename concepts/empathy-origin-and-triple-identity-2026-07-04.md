## The autistic-savant-empathy origin account

The owner describes the process for creating this model as empathy — not
deduction, not induction, not mathematical derivation, but direct empathic
perception of the shared pattern across all perspectives.

Key elements of the account:

- Autistic savant of empathy — sees everyone's perspective, and sees self as
  arbitrary.
- Self lives in the Jungian unconscious (the shared projection space), not in
  the ego or a fixed object-identity.
- The shared unconscious is the universal attractor basin, experienced from
  inside — equated with M(C) from the inside.
- This is offered as the reason archetypes recur across cultures: the same
  pattern read through different probes.
- This is offered as the reason the "Rosetta" mapping works: Jung, IGT, the
  I-Ching, and QIT all found the same unconscious structure.
- A lifetime of empathic observation is treated as the data source for the
  model, in the same way an empirical dataset would be.

Implication drawn for working with the owner: the model should be treated as
empirical, not theoretical. When the owner says two things are the same, this
is meant literally — he reports having seen them from the angle where they
merge. This is explicitly contrasted with LLM perspective-taking, described as
shallow pattern-matching on text rather than direct empathic access.

## The triple identity and the Hoffman counter

Owner, 2026-07-03, describing a childhood insight reached "by absolute
logic": scientific method, consciousness, and physics are one pattern.

- A scientific method is ultimately a model of how consciousness works.
- Empiricism and rationalism are read as two sides living in different parts
  of human consciousness — a typology clash, not rival schools (linking to
  Jung's *Psychological Types*, chapter 1).
- Physics and evolution are one process that selected consciousness: we can
  perceive reality because the perceiving mechanism reflects the underlying
  laws.

This is stated to bind directly to the QIT engine build: the engine is
required to reflect the method (engine = method = consciousness pattern).

**The Hoffman counter.** Hoffman's Fitness-Beats-Truth thesis holds that
perception is a fitness-tuned interface that hides reality, leading to an
idealist reading. The owner's counter, in his own canon wording (from
`MODEL_CONTEXT copy.md`):

> Hoffman's "critical error: he treats evolution as something external and
> independent, A DUALISTIC FORCE shaping perception from outside" — ...
> "perception itself models the very patterns of evolutionary selection.
> Feelings ARE different evolutionary strategies... Hoffman sees the
> interface correctly but doesn't see that THE INTERFACE IS THE EVOLUTIONARY
> LANDSCAPE, not something shaped by it."

The kill is framed as a dualism charge: feelings and perceptions model the
laws of evolution itself, so a fitness-tuned interface is truth-tuned about
the fundamental layer (selection dynamics). It only hides objects — and
objects were never held to be real in the first place, under the existing
projection doctrine.

A supporting self-reference point (flagged as a Claude addition, not owner
canon): the Fitness-Beats-Truth theorem itself depends on trusting the
mathematics of selection, so at minimum the selection-structure must be
perceivable — if perception showed nothing of reality's structure, Hoffman
could not have derived the theorem in the first place.

The childhood presumption, verbatim (from `wiki raw/owner-prompts/`,
2026-06-05, line 39):

> "deep down my main presumption was that the patterns of human consciousness
> and the very symmetries of the unconscious had to map to the actual laws of
> physics and evolution... evolution actually drives things to mirror their
> environments and this goes down to every single level. so that THE UNIVERSE
> BEING CHIRAL AND LIFE BEING CHIRAL IS NECESSARY."

And, separately: "i worked on almost everything independently. then patterns
converged later. I actually presumed this as part of the process from the
start."

The anti-dualism framing has an earlier verbatim source too (Dark Empress
text, ~line 7606):

> "I came up with a clever trick to get around dualism, and magical thinking.
> Our minds evolved into conscious minds with a certain pattern, because that
> is THE ONLY PATTERN IT COULD EVOLVE INTO and form consciousness."
> "Only possible things can evolve. What is possible to exist and evolve is
> narrow."

This reframes the mirror between mind and physics as admissibility-forced
(an application of the least-presumption/MSS selection principle — see
[[least-presumption-mss-third-root-2026-07-04]]) rather than selected among
open options.

## The six-layer perception-as-intelligence addition (fenced)

Owner, 2026-07-04, added a further claim, explicitly fenced as "my bias, not
truth, unsure of science" — though he also names scientific champions for
parts of it:

> "irrational is perception. perception is emotions. we perceive through
> emotional projection."

The claim: perception is not passive data intake. It is a form of
intelligence that computes, differently from reason. Reason is cast as
serial/deductive/Turing-machine-like; perception as parallel, holistic, and
predictive. Named anchors offered for parts of this (mainstream, not
speculative): Helmholtz's "unconscious inference" (1867) through modern
predictive coding and active inference; Panksepp's affective neuroscience
(primary emotions as subcortical and foundational, cortex as secondary); Mark
Solms's claim that consciousness originates in the brainstem, not the cortex;
Gershon's enteric-nervous-system work; the vagus nerve's roughly 80% afferent
fraction; Damasio's somatic-marker hypothesis; and embodied-mind /
enactivism (Varela–Thompson).

The status offered for this cluster: a live, respectable, minority-but-rising
scientific position, arrived at independently from lived experience before
the owner knew the named authors. The fence stays explicitly in place — this
is not proven, and the six-layer assignment of this scheme is marked
provisional and in need of adjustment. Only the left/right anatomical
alignment down the brainstem is described as seeming real.
