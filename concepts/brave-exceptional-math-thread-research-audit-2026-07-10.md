## Claim Ladder

### Established Mathematical Ground

- Finite-dimensional Euclidean Jordan algebras have spectral decompositions,
  permitting entropy functions of eigenvalue vectors.
- The exceptional simple Euclidean Jordan algebra is the 27-dimensional Albert
  algebra of `3 x 3` Hermitian octonionic matrices; its automorphism group is
  the compact real form of `F4`.
- The Cayley plane can be represented through rank-one idempotents in the
  Albert algebra and is related to `F4/Spin(9)`.
- Octonion commutators form a Malcev algebra, and Moufang identities provide
  controlled replacements for some associative manipulations.
- Max-plus/tropical arithmetic, persistent homology, information geometry,
  quantum Fisher metrics, and Hamilton-Jacobi-Bellman methods are genuine but
  logically independent tool families.

### Model-Specific Research Programs

- Todorov and collaborators use octonions, the Albert algebra, and exceptional
  symmetry in Standard Model reconstruction programs. These papers establish
  concrete models and calculations, not unique derivations from the present
  constraint set.
- Exceptional Jordan composite no-go results depend on operational axioms.
  Barnum, Graydon, and Wilce show that under their EJA composite framework no
  composite having an exceptional factor exists. This is narrower and more
  useful than saying exceptional systems simply cannot compose.
- Sedenion and octonion generation-count proposals exist. Their existence does
  not make three generations a universal consequence of nonassociativity.
- Index-theoretic generation counts occur in specified compactifications and
  bundles; changing the geometry or representation changes the result.

### Unsupported Leaps In The Thread

- `entropy = topology` as a literal universal identity;
- gravity derived from nonassociativity without a field equation and empirical
  limit;
- an octonionic dark-matter halo profile without a specified action, dynamics,
  parameter fit, and comparison against baryonic and standard halo controls;
- a physical `E8` entropy cascade inferred from group inclusions;
- identifying the Albert algebra's dimension 27, its three diagonal slots, and
  the three observed fermion generations as the same explanatory fact;
- treating lack of a conventional tensor product as a universal ban on every
  operational composite or Choi-like representation.

## Ratchetable Targets

1. **Exceptional spectral entropy.** Implement positive trace-one Albert
   elements, spectral entropy, perturbation controls, and basis-invariance
   tests using an independent algebra oracle.
2. **Jordan data processing.** Declare one class of positive trace-preserving
   Jordan maps and test relative-entropy monotonicity. A counterexample is a
   successful red result, not something to tune away.
3. **Composite boundary.** Encode the exact Barnum-Graydon-Wilce assumptions
   and test which proposed Lev/QIT composition operation violates which axiom.
4. **Malcev/Moufang dynamics.** Compare a law-preserving nonassociative update
   against corrupted multiplication tables and associative projections on a
   held-out prediction or control task.
5. **Generation claims.** Require a fixed representation, operator, index, and
   perturbation family before counting. Dimension numerology is a kill control.
6. **Dark-sector claims.** Require a dynamical action, generated profile,
   preregistered observational data, conventional halo baselines, baryonic
   nuisance models, and out-of-sample likelihood comparison.
7. **Cross-view object formation.** Use topology, Jordan spectra, tropical
   projections, graph invariants, and predictive behavior as independent views
   of the same finite object registry. Shared labels are forbidden evidence;
   convergence must be recovered from measurements.

## Current Verdict

The thread does expose a coherent research basin: finite predictive objects can
be probed through geometry, entropy, algebra, topology, dynamics, and
computation. It does not establish that these views are one mathematical object
or that the QIT schedule follows from them. That convergence is the experiment.
The Ratchet must earn it by recovering the same object partition and useful
held-out work across independently implemented views.
