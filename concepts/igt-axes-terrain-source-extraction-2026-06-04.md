### 720-Degree Spinor Loop

No source doc in the requested set explicitly gives a 720-degree spinor-loop law or a pass/fail invariant for it. The ingredients are present: spinor carrier `S^3`, Weyl sheets, Hopf loops, and Pauli rotations. But a 720-degree claim would require a new explicit SU(2)/spinor double-cover test: positive result = sign/phase behavior changes under 360 degrees and returns under 720 degrees in the finite carrier while controls erase it; negative result = no distinction from ordinary 2pi periodic scalar loop or no load-bearing effect on the relevant observable.
