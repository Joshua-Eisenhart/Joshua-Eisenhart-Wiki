The owner treats F01 (finitude) and N01 (noncommutation) as root axioms — the ground itself — not as constraints layered on top of classical mathematics. This is a different move from mainstream constraint engineering (SAT/SMT/verification), which filters pre-existing classical objects. F01+N01 aim to rebuild the ground those objects would sit on.

## F01 and N01 as root, not filter

F01 (finitude) and N01 (noncommutation) are not constraints applied to a pre-existing classical universe of sets, functions, and real numbers. They are proposed as the ground itself. Classical structures — commutative rings, completed infinities, symmetric tensors — appear only as higher-layer patterns that survive bounded, ordered probing. They are never given as starting material.

## The 11 consequences (owner-authored)

1. **Infinity is excluded from foundation.** Limits, completeness, the real numbers, and the naturals-as-completed-infinite cannot be ground. They may appear as patterns that survive bounded probing at higher layers, never as ground. This excludes most of 19th- and 20th-century mathematics from the base.

2. **Commutativity is excluded from foundation.** Abelian groups, commutative rings, and symmetric tensors are not ground. Order-dependent structures are ground; commutativity is a special case that survives certain probes.

3. **Time-structure becomes primitive.** Order is substrate, not a layer on top of an atemporal substrate. Reversible evolution — classical mechanics, unitary quantum mechanics — is a limit case where N01 becomes invisible over small windows, not a foundational fact.

4. **The external observer is forbidden.** F01 rules out the infinite-scope outside view. Classical scientific realism — an independent reality described from outside — fails at root.

5. **Objects are emergent, never primitive.** No infinity means no primitive defined by an infinite condition. No commutativity means no primitive defined by order-independent properties. Objects are patterns that survived enough bounded, ordered probes. They are always derived.

6. **Truth is temporal and local.** "X is true" reduces to "X survived this check at this step." There is no atemporal truth-value. Excluded middle is not ground; sequential, linear, temporal logic is closer.

7. **Computation becomes the natural substrate.** Finite plus ordered is computation, literally. Turing machines are almost F01+N01-native. Mathematics and physics must re-derive to match this, not the other way round.

8. **Operating systems, distributed systems, and databases all re-ground.** Under F01+N01, every operation is a bounded, ordered patch to a graph of prior patches. Lev OS gestures this way already, with receipts, patches, and planes; the pure form goes all the way down.

9. **Identity is probe-local.** "Same thing at two times" holds only within a probe family that cannot distinguish them. Across probe families, identity fractures. Hume's bundle theory falls out mechanically.

10. **Meaning is practice, not reference.** Without an abstract substrate behind labels, meaning is the pattern of checks the label has survived. Operationalism and pragmatism follow as consequences, not as extra commitments.

11. **Receipts become the primary mathematical object.** Not propositions, not sets, not functions — certificates of bounded, ordered checks. Under F01+N01, mathematics is receipt algebra: what certificates compose, what checks they license, what further receipts they admit.

## Receipts as the primary object

Point 11 carries operational weight beyond the list. Canonical sim outputs — certificates, witness traces, UNSAT proofs — ARE the mathematical objects in this framing, not decorations on top of a "real" underlying truth. Status labels (SURVIVED / KILLED / OPEN) follow directly from point 6: there is no atemporal truth to report against, only what has survived a check at a given step. Probe-local identity (point 9) is why `a = a iff a ~ b` is treated as a root axiom rather than a theorem to be derived.

## Why this is not mainstream constraint engineering

SAT, SMT, formal verification, and optimisation all sit on top of classical foundations. They use constraints to filter pre-existing objects — sets, functions, real numbers, commutative operations — that are already assumed to exist.

F01+N01 go underneath that layer. They aim to rebuild the ground the constraints are supposed to run on. The constraints are not a sieve over classical math; under this doctrine they ARE the foundation, and classical math appears only as a higher-layer pattern that happens to survive.

## Applying this in research, sims, and docs

- Any "foundation" proposal that starts from the real numbers, limits, a completed infinity, or an abelian structure is off-surface at root.
- Any sim whose ground includes commutativity or infinity as given is a classical-baseline probe at best, not canonical.
- The target endgame is math and physics rebuilt so F01+N01 are ground and classical structures appear as survivor layers. "World engine," in the owner's end state, names this rebuild.
- The OS and distributed-systems reframe (point 8) is why Leviathan OS is treated as the natural systems-engineering home for this math; they share roots.
