# Tropical And Max-Plus Placement Research - 2026-07-10

## Research Status

Hypothesis and tool-placement page. This is not Ratchet canon.

## What The Mathematics Is

The max-plus semiring replaces ordinary addition with `max` and ordinary
multiplication with addition. Tropical and idempotent mathematics study the
geometry and analysis induced by such semirings.

[Litvinov's Maslov-dequantization introduction](https://arxiv.org/abs/math/0507014)
places tropical mathematics as a dequantized or limiting arithmetic. That is a
real mathematical relation, but it does not mean a particular QIT engine must
contain a tropical rung.

Recent work also uses the arithmetic operationally. [Tropical
Attention](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.17190) constructs attention native to the
max-plus semiring for dynamic-programming-style algorithmic reasoning, and
[Min-Max-Plus Neural Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2102.06358) develops
trainable tropical layers. These are potential computational tools for
deductive search, value propagation, and graph control surfaces.

## What Packet 110 Shows

Packet 110 compares bare max-plus monotonicity with equal-amplitude coherent
interference. The coherent intensity can fall below either single intensity;
an incoherent probability sum cannot. This supports only:

```text
bare max is not a direct representation of this coherent amplitude combiner
```

It does not show:

- that every tropical representation fails to encode quantum behavior;
- that tropical mathematics is absent from higher or classical-limit layers;
- that `T -> 0`, Bellman, or oracle-duality placement is forced;
- that a tropical engine stage exists.

## Useful Leviathan/Engine Roles

The strongest practical hypothesis is computational, not foundational:

- max-plus for shortest/longest path and dynamic-programming projections;
- tropical attention for algorithmic graph tasks in Lev meshes;
- a classical-limit control against the QIT carrier;
- a candidate compression/value-propagation substrate for blue-collar SME
  workflow graphs.

Any such use needs an external task benchmark where the tropical lane beats a
standard baseline and an ablation removes that advantage. Package presence or
the word `Bellman` is not enough.
