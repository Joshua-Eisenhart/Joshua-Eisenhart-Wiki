# FEP, Autopoiesis, Enactivism, and Sense-Making

This page keeps the life/cognition neighbor concepts visible without letting them become hidden proof.

## Neighbor concepts

| Concept | Plain role | Wiki use | Guardrail |
|---|---|---|---|
| autopoiesis | self-production and self-maintenance | persistence and boundary support | not implementation proof |
| enactivism | cognition as embodied world-coupled activity | world-coupling language | not a formal derivation |
| sense-making | environment matters through organism-relative significance | viability and policy language | not subjective proof |
| adaptivity | regulation against viability loss | survival-under-constraints | not teleology |
| active inference | inference/action under FEP | planning and prediction support | not Leviathan implementation |

## Fit

These concepts help explain why boundary, action, perception, and survival should not be split into unrelated modules. They also make the holodeck/world-model pages less like static memory and more like an active relation between model, world, and viability.

## Tension

FEP often formalizes life/cognition in statistical and dynamical terms. Enactive and autopoietic traditions often emphasize embodied autonomy, normativity, and lived sense-making. The wiki should preserve that tension instead of pretending one fully contains the other.

## Blocked use

- Do not use autopoiesis as evidence of consciousness.
- Do not use enactivism as proof of Codex Ratchet ontology.
- Do not claim Leviathan implements sense-making from conceptual overlap.
- Do not let broad life-language promote Axis0, QIT, or physics claims.
