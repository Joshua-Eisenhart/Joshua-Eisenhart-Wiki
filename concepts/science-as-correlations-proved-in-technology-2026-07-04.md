## Correlations vs stories

- (d) Science is the correlations. Causal stories are compression narratives.
- (d) Correlations accumulate; stories churn and are supplanted.
- (s) This splits Kuhn's picture: the cumulative layer is correlation, the
  revolutionary layer is story — so both "science accumulates" and "paradigms
  break" hold, of different layers.
- (t) Structural realism (Worrall) preserves mathematical structure across
  theory change; ontic structural realism (Ladyman, *Every Thing Must Go*)
  pushes structure below object-first ontology — the academy's route toward
  "structure, not story."
- (t) Pearl's do-calculus computes correlations under intervention
  distributions; (s) the do-operator reads here as a probe-family choice, so
  "X causes Y" cashes out as "the X-Y correlation is stable under the
  intervention probe."

## Technology as the closure

- (d) Technology is where science is corroborated with no interpretive escape:
  when the chip, bridge, clock, drug, or navigation stack works, the captured
  correlation was good enough. (s) This is the Duhem-Quine escape closed by an
  operational gate rather than an argument.
- (t) GPS operationally validates both relativities continuously — a standing
  recomputation at scale, not a one-time test.
- (d) Technology is ontology-agnostic and correlation-loyal: (s) operational
  systems track reliable correlations while staying agnostic about ultimate
  ontology — bridges did not fall when Newton's ontology was superseded, which
  is what a nominalist expects.
- (s) Field-health corollary: fields that ship instruments stay alive
  (neurology, sequencing); fields that ship none stall — a usable BS-detector,
  stated as synthesis.
