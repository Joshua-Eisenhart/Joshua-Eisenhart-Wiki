# Doubly Quantum Mechanics

## Overview
This is a cautious support note for a speculative arXiv paper that proposes a modification of quantum mechanics in which the geometrical configurations themselves acquire quantum features. It is not a settled part of the wiki's backbone. It is a candidate extension worth tracking because it explicitly tries to alter the structure of the geometry rather than merely the state space.

## Abstract-level summary
The paper's abstract says the motivation comes from quantum gravity: relativistic symmetries may acquire quantum features. The proposed framework, "Doubly Quantum Mechanics," is a modification of quantum mechanics in which the geometrical configurations are quantum. The paper presents initial steps rather than a finished theory.

## Why it may matter here
- It is a direct candidate for the wiki's geometry-first / nonclassical support lane.
- It may help distinguish ordinary quantum state structure from a more radical quantum-geometry extension.
- It is relevant only as an exploratory extension, not as a current governing framework.
