## Three things "entangled rolling dice" names — do not blur them

The phrase carries a picture, a mechanism, and a physical claim. This page separates them — the split is curatorial (the sources support each part but do not state it in exactly this three-way form):

| Aspect | What it is | Status in the model |
|---|---|---|
| Dice (spinning/rolling) | Metaphor for space=possibility, time=the seen roll | Pure metaphor — fenced from sim design |
| Entropy gradient (Axis 0) | Proposed mechanism the metaphor points at | Speculative mechanism, not earned at engine scale |
| Entanglement | Proposed simplest structure that evolves + preserves info | Load-bearing hypothesis (owner-stated), not measured |


1. **The dice = a METAPHOR / picture.** A universe-sized die spinning forever is space — nothingness, all possibilities at once; when it settles on one face, time begins ([[legacy-physics-cosmology]], from the Grandmaster: "Space is possibility. Time is the observation of the result of a dice roll"; "a spinning dice presumes less than a rolled dice"). Owner binding correction (2026-07-04): the rolling dice is a metaphor and must never be mechanized into a sim design.
2. **The mechanism = the innate entropy gradient (Axis 0).** What the dice-picture points at is the entropy gradient present at the beginning — the tension between positive entropy (growth/expansion/possibility) and negative entropy (records/locks/preserved structure). A ratchet cannot move without a gradient; the gradient is what the metaphor stands in for. This is a binding owner correction carried in current wiki context (2026-07-04), not a claim of the Grandmaster/cosmology source docs; see [[geometry-entropy-dual-ratchet-verdict-2026-07-04]] and [[least-presumption-mss-third-root-2026-07-04]].
3. **The physical claim = entanglement as the simplest evolvable structure.** Distinct from the metaphor and stated by the owner as "stronger than a metaphor": entanglement itself is probably what created the universe, because it is the simplest possible structure that can evolve while preserving information ([[sequential-universe-spinor-network-physics-model]]:80). This is a load-bearing claim, which is why spinors and chirality are central rather than decorative.
