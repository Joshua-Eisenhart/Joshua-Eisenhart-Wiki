## PCA Digest
PCA is a linear method that uses covariance, extracts orthogonal principal directions, and keeps the largest-variance eigenmodes. It is a compression / denoising / visualization tool.

## QPCA Digest
QPCA is the quantum-algorithm analogue of PCA. Classic paper: Lloyd, Mohseni, Rebentrost (arXiv:1307.0401). It targets dominant spectral structure of a density operator or covariance operator. Later work emphasizes state-preparation cost assumptions and limitations.

## Alignment Labels
- PCA -> covariance eigen-decomposition
- QPCA -> quantum-access spectral extraction
- Schmidt decomposition -> bipartite principal-mode extraction
- low-rank density approximation -> valid compression primitive
