## Main points
- Replaces traditional governance with delegated proxy voting and transparent logging.
- Treats semantic AI and natural-language intent handling as a core platform layer.
- Uses mirrors, feedback loops, and self-reflection as development mechanisms.
- Emphasizes family, localism, merit, and accountability as governing principles.
- Framed as scalable from local cooperatives to cosmic-scale civilization.
- Defines the framework explicitly as the "Recursive Freedom Framework" and ties governance, economics, development mirrors, and semantic computing into one operating system.
- Adds specific currencies and institutional components: Social Benefit Currency, Cooperative Resource Currency, Ecosystem Contribution Currency, Paladin centers, and Ugly Mirror development infrastructure.
- Makes transparency and revocable hierarchy operational through delegated proxy voting, veto powers, and blockchain-style logging rather than keeping them as abstract ideals.
- Treats recursive scientific methodology as a load-bearing core, not a side note: mirrors, feedback loops, embedded observers, and empirical refinement are central to the framework's own method.
- In the deeper integrated wiki reading now preserved elsewhere, Leviathan's science method is treated as one candidate surface of the same recursive engine logic that later appears in the holodeck, FEP-style predictive loops, and the QIT engine program.

## Legacy source specifics
The newly ingested Leviathan v3.2 doc clarifies several details that were only implicit before:
- the framework is explicitly pitched as a decentralized social operating system rather than just a governance manifesto
- semantic AI / Leviathan OS is a first-class platform layer, not a sidecar tool
- development mirrors exist at individual, organizational, and ecosystem scale
- the framework includes a concrete multi-currency layer for services, resources, and ecosystem projects
- the framework openly centers attractiveness, hierarchy, localism, and family structure as stabilizing principles

These details make Leviathan a stronger bridge between [[the-dark-empress]] and later wiki runtime/governance surfaces: the Dark Empress carries the civilizational-build and colonization energy, while Leviathan v3.2 sharpens that into a named operating system with explicit institutions, currencies, mirrors, and recursive methodology.

## Legacy book connection
The Dark Empress is one of the clearer upstream containers for the civilization-scaling side of this framework. Its future-building sections include galactic colonization, station law, colony design, AI parents, online education, money, banking, and open finance. That makes it an important genealogy source for why later Leviathan material mixes governance, infrastructure, human development, and civilizational scaling in one surface rather than treating them as separate policy domains.
