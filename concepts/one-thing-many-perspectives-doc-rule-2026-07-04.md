## Overview

The owner does not build systems from separate components stitched together. The method is to find things that are maximally correlated, then rotate perspective until they resolve into one object. This is not reduction and not unification-by-stitching — it is perspective rotation until multiplicity becomes unity.

## The method

Take a set of highly correlated things. Find the perspective where they are the same thing. Do this without presuming the underlying assumptions that usually keep them separate — in particular, without presuming causality and universals. Owner, in his own words (2026-07-03): "i observe reality. i look at what is most empirically real. then i just put them all together, and find patterns... take a set of highly correlated things, then find the perspective where they are the same. and just don't presume the underlying presumptions going into things. by not presuming causality and universals, one is free to see the other options."

Highly correlated things should be treated as potentially identical, not merely linked.

## The physics instance

Gravity, dark energy, time, space, entropy, and entanglement are one thing, seen from six angles. Under a causality frame, these show no pattern — there is no single one of them "causing" another. The rotation itself is what makes them resolve into one.

Other instances the owner names as the same pattern from different angles:

- cosmological argument, ontological ratchet, teleological selection
- retrocausality, jk fuzz, i-scalar, gravity model
- Axis 0, constraint manifold, FEP prediction-first
- ratchet functionals, Boltzmann brain, stochastic retrocausal selection
- negative feedback / positive feedback = yin / yang = homeostasis / allostasis = Type 1 / Type 2
- bell curve (negative-feedback dominated) vs power law (positive-feedback dominated), marking where the ratchet engages
- chirality leading to statistical patterns, power laws, ratcheting evolution, and on to everything
- the standard model particle zoo as which symmetries survived the attractor basin (proposed, maximally falsifiable)

## Foundational commitment

Statistics and symmetry are the universe's fundamentals, not causality. Causality is what you see when you pick one perspective and freeze it into a narrative.

The process needs both discipline and openness at once. Owner: "the skepticism also needs radical imagination inside high constraints. use logic and empiricism to their max, doesn't mean to just follow what others say is true." A spinning dice, which presumes less than a rolled dice, is the emblem for this.

## The operational documentation rule

This method has a direct consequence for how documents in this wiki should be written:

- Do not organise the owner's ideas as separate topics that merely relate to each other.
- They are one topic, described from different measurement angles.
- When the owner lists several things, ask: what is the single thing these are all perspectives on?
- A document should present one core claim, then perspectives on it — not separate sections for separate ideas.
