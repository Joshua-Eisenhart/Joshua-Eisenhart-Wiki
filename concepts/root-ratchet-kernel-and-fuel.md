# Root ratchet kernel and fuel

Research-note tier. The kernel is explicitly agnostic: "It is not the owner's
physics model, the candidate grammar, a theorem prover, or Leviathan" (pack
177 v2, `ROOT_RATCHET_KERNEL.md`).

## The K_r tick law

At tick `r`, freeze a state object:

```
K_r = (D_r, G_r, Π_r, {⪯_i}, C_r, H_r)
```

— `D_r` finite typed distinction/continuation records currently available;
`G_r` the replaceable finite exploration grammar; `Π_r` executable probes and
measurements; `{⪯_i}` explicit, plural presumption orders; `C_r` frozen
controls and claim ceilings; `H_r` immutable prior receipts, failures, and
retained context (`ROOT_RATCHET_KERNEL.md`). The tick itself:

```
P_r = G_r(D_r, H_r)              -- proposals
M_r(p) = Π_r(p; D_r)             -- measurement
A_r = { p ∈ P_r : C_r(p, M_r(p)) }   -- admitted
F_{r,i} = Min_{⪯_i}(A_r),  F_r = {F_{r,i}}_i    -- per-order minima
```

"Incomparable minima remain alive. No hidden scalar chooses one ontology"
(same file). This is the formal ground for "plural minima" as a standing
property of the kernel, not an occasional edge case.

## Pawl rules

The pawl carries retained direction through receipts and exclusions, **not**
by forcing survivor sets to monotonically shrink. Required (`ROOT_RATCHET_KERNEL.md`,
"The pawl"):

- no admitted claim without an executable witness and its controls;
- no silent weakening of a gate, grammar, bound, or claim ceiling;
- no erasure of failed candidates or countermodels;
- no source-size weighting disguised as recurrence;
- no selector access to source names or owner-hypothesis labels;
- re-evaluation after a material context change;
- a deletion/countermodel witness for every claimed load-bearing constraint.

"Candidates may return from Purgatory and prior winners may fail. What cannot
move backward silently is the record of what was tested, under which frozen
context, and why its status changed" (same file). Purgatory is thus a named
holding state for candidates that are neither admitted nor deleted-forever —
parked, with an explicit re-offer path when the context fingerprint
materially changes: "every parked candidate is eligible for re-offer... The
full boundary is then recomputed; old summary statistics are not treated as
the new raw boundary" (same file, main tick description).

## The residual vector Δ = (c,k,u,g)

Held-out records, controls, and continuation probes generate a residual
compiled into the next obligation, `O_{r+1} = Compile(Δ_r, C_r, H_r)`. "If
Δ_r supplies no new finite obligation, the process holds. It does not
manufacture a next step to keep a narrative moving" (`ROOT_RATCHET_KERNEL.md`).
The residual is retained as a vector, never collapsed to one search score:

```
Δ_r = (c_r, k_r, u_r, g_r)
```

Per pack 177 v2's `FUEL_MECHANISM.md` (§1, "Operational fuel: measured
residual pressure"), source by source:

- **c** — contexts the current returned coordinates identify even though
  their observed continuations differ;
- **k** — candidates killed by later held-out records;
- **u** — plural surviving completions or plural minimal quotients;
- **g** — missing or failed source-runtime interfaces.

"Each nonzero component generates a typed obligation, and a fair scheduler
eventually offers every obligation" (same file). The finite root analogue of
"entropy escaping": `x ~_r y` but `Λ_h(x) ≠ Λ_h(y)` — the current quotient
treats two occurrences alike while an admitted finite continuation exposes a
difference. This operational residual is explicitly **not** automatically
thermodynamic entropy; the owner's physical entropy-gradient hypothesis is
kept in a separate reservoir (`FUEL_MECHANISM.md`, §1).

## The three fuels

Pack 177 v1 "made a real finite calculation, but it let that small
calibration become the center of the project. That was a semantic deletion.
The Ratchet needs three different fuels, and they must remain separate"
(`FUEL_MECHANISM.md`, opening):

1. **Operational fuel** — measured residual pressure, `Δ_r = (c_r,k_r,u_r,g_r)`
   above; generated by counterexample or unresolved distinction, never by
   narrative choice.
2. **Proposal fuel** — a plural construction ecology: existing repo sims and
   negative results, finite relations/automata/constraint systems/tilings,
   ordered/bracketed histories, spinor/Clifford/division-algebra/spectral/
   nonassociative carriers, finite Feynman/Dirac constructions, QIT/FEP/
   JEPA-like predictive models, biological convergence problems, and the
   owner's proposed physics/engine architecture. "These entries are fuel
   because they reduce blind combinatorial search. They do not become
   evidence merely by being proposed" (§2). Tracked in `fuel/FUEL_REGISTRY.json`
   with role, assumption boundary, executable surface, and evidence license
   per entry — no hidden priority score.
3. **Owner-hypothesis fuel** — the owner's hypotheses as "unusually detailed
   candidate structures," kept explicit in `OWNER_HYPOTHESIS_FUEL_MAP.md`
   rather than hidden: "Hiding them is not agnosticism; it destroys proposal
   fuel and guarantees rediscovery by accident" (§3). They may seed typed
   candidates and sealed comparisons, but their labels, desired dimensions,
   stage counts, physics names, and symbolic correspondences are "forbidden
   from the mathematical selector."

`source/compile_fuel_obligations.py` is the fair-consumption interface: it
reads already-executed anonymous r3-r6 receipts, compiles a residual vector
and concrete obligations per source, and emits a round-robin queue for
Leviathan — "The queue order is logistical only. Leviathan may schedule,
resume, event, and receipt this queue; it may not decide mathematical truth"
(`FUEL_MECHANISM.md`, "Fair consumption"). Every proposal lane stays typed
`PROPOSE`, `RUN`, `HOLD`, `FAIL`, or `PARK` until its own execution changes
that state.

## Nested constraint and the "whole manifold acts as one" law

`ROOT_RATCHET_KERNEL.md`'s nested-tower formalism is the same structure named
on the literal-GCM page ([[concepts/literal-entropic-gcm]]): for a candidate
tower `T_r = {(x_0,...,x_L) : ∧_i C_i(x≤i) ∧ ∧_i R_i(x_i,x_{i+1}) ∧ H_r(x≤L)}`,
the survivor at a layer is a projection of the complete compatible tower,
`X*_{i,r} = π_i(T_r)` — "enclosing layers constrain their contents and inner
extendability constrains which enclosing layers survive. This is a kernel
capability, not an admission of any particular shell, spinor, Hopf, engine,
or cosmological hypothesis" (`ROOT_RATCHET_KERNEL.md`, "Nested constraint").
Pack 177 v2 explicitly disclaims completeness: it "executes one bounded
slice of this kernel over returned (H,P,O) projections. It does not claim
that this slice is the complete root Ratchet," and separately executes the
missing residual-to-obligation compiler — the owner's physical
entropy-gradient hypothesis is a high-value proposal about how a universe
may instantiate this architecture, "not silently equated with the kernel's
finite residual pressure" (same file, closing paragraph).

## Pack 181 supersession: "the ratchet can always ratchet"

Pack 181's binding owner correction (`OWNER_CORRECTION_AND_SUPERSESSION.md`)
states the newly explicit governing rule verbatim:

> The Ratchet can always ratchet. It always has a defaulted thing it has
> ratcheted. Later work can loop back, find something that better meets the
> requirements, or add nuance. It cannot prove an absolute MSS; it can only
> compare things. The nesting itself and the order of that nesting change and
> constrain things. The whole manifold acts as one, with every layer acting
> on its neighbors — down and up, in and out.

This is stated to govern Pack 181's active process, and it explicitly
**supersedes** (same file, "What it supersedes"):

- waiting at `NO_RUNG` until an absolute first minimal structure is proved;
- treating the base or any later layer as permanently finished;
- a fixed linear ladder whose order is external to the candidates;
- a fixed candidate grammar whose inability to respond becomes a terminal
  `HOLD` rather than a reason to alter the candidate structure;
- treating a surviving count as the identity of the surviving mathematics;
- using one `settle()` result when different finite calculation orders
  produce different fixed points or cycles;
- calling the latest winner the truth rather than the operational default;
- treating entropy, a Hessian, QIT, QCA, spinors, engines, or owner physics
  as universal Ratchet assumptions.

Earlier work is explicitly **not deleted** under this correction: the
`1771→121→11` restriction (packs 184/185) is retained as a useful operation
test, with Pack 181 itself proving "the same counts can arise from two
different equations and different state sets, so the counts alone are not a
Ratchet tooth"; QCA v6 remains a strong bounded source/verification lane;
Pack 180 remains a bounded QIT evidence return with a repaired portable
verifier boundary in Pack 181; Pack 179 remains the full cumulative
conceptual/executable baseline; Leviathan remains execution/event/graph/
receipt infrastructure that "does not choose mathematical winners" (same
file, "Status of earlier work"). "No result is absolute canon" afterward — an
admission is scoped to the exact candidates compared, requirements used,
nesting/connections used, evidence/calculation orders used, and the exact
witness by which it beat another candidate: "the receipt is permanent; the
winner is not" (same file, "Meaning of 'canon' after this correction").

## Open / unresolved — the 177-vs-181 relationship

Pack 177 v2 and Pack 181 are not shown, in the files read for this page, to
be in direct conflict — 177 v2's kernel/fuel/pawl machinery is a
lower-level, still-standing apparatus, and 181's correction operates one
level up, on how *results from* that apparatus may be treated (scoped,
revisable, never absolute canon). But the two packs do differ in emphasis
and were produced only a day apart under evident time pressure (177 v2:
2026-07-18; 181: 2026-07-18/19), and 177 v2's own text records that its
immediate predecessor (177 v1) had already once compressed the project's
fuel away by over-centering one finite calculation — the same failure mode
181's correction is written to guard against at the process level. Whether
181's "no absolute MSS, only comparison" framing requires any revision to
177 v2's `Δ_r` residual vector, its pawl rules, or its fuel-registry
mechanics is not addressed by any file read here, and is held open rather
than resolved.
