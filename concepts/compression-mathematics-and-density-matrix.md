# Compression Mathematics and Density Matrix

## Overview
This page captures the explicit mathematical route from compression to spectral truncation, rate-distortion, PCA, kernel PCA, and quantum rate-distortion.

## Main points
- Compression is low-rank approximation of a density matrix.
- Rate-distortion and water-filling appear as spectral truncation problems.
- Quantum compression uses typical subspaces and Schumacher compression.
- PCA and kernel PCA are special cases of the same spectral idea.
