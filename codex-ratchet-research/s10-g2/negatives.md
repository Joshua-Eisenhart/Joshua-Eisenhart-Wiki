# S10 G2 - Negatives

Status: populated by corpus wave 2 child lane on 2026-06-11.

This file records what classification and current local evidence forbid. Treat
these as kill tests and overclaim traps for future S10 packets.

## Classification and analogy traps

- `G2` as a root-system label does not force an octonion carrier. The local
  forced-vs-installed discriminator says the compact G2 evidence is installed
  by carrier constraints, not forced by bare root constraints. Local source:
  `system_v6/receipts/s10_g2_family_mine_20260610.md`.
- Compact and split labels are not interchangeable. Any row saying only "G2"
  without real form and metric/signature is S10-insufficient. Sources: Draper,
  arXiv:1704.07819; local S10 family mine.
- The split real-form label `G2(2)` must not be confused with the finite
  Chevalley group `G2(2)`. Future finite-group rows need separate packet ids.
  Local source: `system_v6/receipts/s10_g2_family_mine_20260610.md`.
- D4 triality is not a proof of compact G2, split G2, or a physical
  three-generation claim. It is a route label that requires explicit D4 bases,
  maps, and residual/order checks for any local packet. Sources: Baez
  arXiv:math/0105155; McRae arXiv:2502.14016; local
  `geo_s10_intertwiner_depth_v0` audit.
- Fano incidence does not itself prove `Der(O)=14`. It is a multiplication-table
  substrate. Derivation/stabilizer dimensions must be recomputed from the table
  actually used. Sources: Sevennec arXiv:1106.6015; local ratchet G2 audit.

## Null-row kill tests

- Kill "full null-line parabolic classification" unless the packet classifies
  all relevant null lines/parabolics under stated real-form hypotheses. Current
  local evidence is a computed null-vector stabilizer row only. Local source:
  `system_v6/sims/geo_s10_intertwiner_depth_v0/audit_verdict.md`.
- Kill "explicit Levi factor" unless a closed embedded complement is
  constructed and bracket-checked. Current local evidence earns nilradical plus
  Levi quotient; one selected complement failed exact closure and only closed
  modulo the radical. Local source:
  `system_v6/sims/geo_s10_intertwiner_depth_v0/audit_verdict.md`.
- Kill compact-to-split stabilizer copy-paste. Compact chosen-unit stabilizer
  rows may use `SU(3)` after unit/metric checks; split rows must classify the
  vector's causal type and solve their own stabilizer equations. Local sources:
  `system_v6/sims/ratchet_g2_family_v0/audit_verdict.md` and
  `system_v6/receipts/s10_g2_family_mine_20260610.md`.
- Kill null-row promotion if the row has dimension/nilpotent counts but no
  bracket, quotient, Killing/signature, and control evidence.

## 480-table overclaim traps

- Kill "480 G2 objects" wording. The 480 count is table-orientation context,
  not 480 promoted objects. A future row should enumerate tables, hashes, orbit
  keys, and representatives before running full invariant packets.
- Kill any 480 claim whose only support is the MathOverflow pointer. Use that
  pointer to design an enumerator; require local exact count and duplicate/orbit
  ledger for packet evidence. Pointer:
  https://mathoverflow.net/questions/131167/octonions-and-the-fano-plane.
- Kill label-only table comparisons. A raw `e_i` basis label is not invariant
  across Bloch-root, MCT-weld, or new orientation artifacts unless the map,
  sign vector, and lift rule are named. Local source:
  `system_v6/receipts/s10_g2_family_mine_20260610.md`.

## Required negative controls

- Corrupted octonion product: should break compact `Der(O)=14`, locally observed
  as a dimension drop to `3` in current receipts.
- Wrong real form/signature: compact metric must fail split signature rows, and
  split rows must fail division/positive-norm claims.
- Erased or sign-flipped Fano line: must alter derivation/stabilizer or
  preservation evidence in the expected direction.
- Wrong identity or wrong D4 representation map: must fail the triality
  intertwiner residual check.
- Permuted projector or table-prose branch echo: must fail branch sums if the
  projector/action rank evidence is erased.
- Nothing-excluded control: where used, should preserve exact hash/dimension and
  prove the negative control is not a broad mutation artifact.

## Claim ceiling

Allowed: label, discriminator, table candidate, standard source pointer,
nilradical-plus-Levi-quotient wording, route-local triality realization, and
scratch diagnostic.

Forbidden without future receipts: physical G2 object, Standard Model claim,
bridge or axis claim, canonical manifold claim, formal admission, crowned
compact/split form, full null-parabolic classification, explicit embedded Levi
factor, and 480 promoted objects.
