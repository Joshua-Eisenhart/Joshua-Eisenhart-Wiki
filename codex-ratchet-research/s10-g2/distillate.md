# S10 G2 - Distillate

Status: populated by corpus wave 2 child lane on 2026-06-11.

MMM register only. Use names as labels. Do not reify abstractions. Do not
promote classification labels into objects.

## Register rules

- Say "this row excludes..." before "this object is...".
- Say "under this table/signature/vector choice..." before "G2".
- Say "label candidate" when the packet has not recomputed the invariant.
- Say "nilradical plus Levi quotient" unless an embedded closed Levi factor is
  actually constructed.
- Say "D4 triality route" unless the packet has explicit bases, maps, and
  residual/order checks.
- Say "480 orientation-table context" unless an enumerator receipt exists.
- Keep compact, split, finite-group, stabilizer, branch, and triality labels in
  separate rows.

- No formal admission.
- No bridge, axis, Standard Model, or physical-object claim.
- No crowned compact/split form.
