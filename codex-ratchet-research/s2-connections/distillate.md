# S2 Connections - Distillate

Use only this file, not the full corpus notes, when a future card or blind sheet
needs an MMM-register extraction.

## Bounded Extraction

`S2 connection` names a tested tuple, not a reified object. Under the round-3
canonicalizer, a candidate is the observed tuple of curvature, Chern class,
lifted leaf holonomies, annular fluxes, and cover-period convention.

`same c1` means topology survived one row. It does not mean same connection.

`same curvature` means the local density row survived. It does not mean the
pinned lifted holonomy row survived.

`leaf match` means the selected leaves did not separate. It does not mean the
expanded spectrum or annular rows are silent.

## Exclusion Language

- Exclude alias only by exact canonical tuple equality.
- Exclude topology-only identity when holonomy or annular flux differs.
- Exclude flux comparisons if the leaf-conditioning rule is not valid.
- Keep promotion blocked: this distillate feeds future cards; it is not result
  evidence and does not authorize uniqueness language.
