# S9 Transport - Distillate

Use only this bounded extraction for future cards or blind sheets.

## Bounded Extraction

`S9 transport` names a tested tuple: simplified `f`, curvature density, Chern
class, leaf holonomy vector, annular flux vector, validity class, and
path-ordered signature when scoped.

`same c1` means topology survived. It does not mean same transport.

`same leaf` means one sampled loop survived. It does not mean the spectrum or
annuli survived.

`path ordered` means the multiplication order is part of the observation.

## Exclusion Language

- Exclude connection identity when only `c1` matches.
- Exclude global holonomy identity from one- or two-leaf matches.
- Exclude transport comparison when noncommuting loops are treated as unordered
  coefficients.
- Keep promotion blocked: this distillate feeds future cards and does not
  authorize global uniqueness language.
