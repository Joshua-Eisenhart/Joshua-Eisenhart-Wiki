# S4 Operators - Distillate

Use only this bounded extraction for future cards or blind sheets.

## Bounded Extraction

`S4 channel` names a tested affine role row: `(role_id, M, c, Choi class,
fixed-axis set)`.

`CPTP` means the channel-validity row survived. It does not mean the row is the
committed role.

`unital` means `c=0`. It excludes amplitude damping-like drift but does not
identify a unique Pauli channel.

`axis relabel` means a convention candidate. It becomes alias only if the parent
probe role and N01 row are preserved by the documented map.

## Exclusion Language

- Exclude positive-only maps by Choi negativity.
- Exclude Pauli-channel identity when `c != 0`.
- Exclude axis-alias claims when the z-probe role moves.
- Keep promotion blocked: this is research extraction only, not proof of S4
  uniqueness or result admission.
