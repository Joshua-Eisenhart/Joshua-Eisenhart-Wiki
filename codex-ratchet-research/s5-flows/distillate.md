# S5 Flows - Distillate

Use only this bounded extraction for future cards or blind sheets.

## Bounded Extraction

`S5 flow` names an observed eight-row affine-generator tuple. The tuple records
`terrain_id`, exact `A`, exact `b`, validity, fixed point, mirror class, and N01
signature.

`same fixed point` means one row survived. It does not mean same transient flow.

`same quotient` means the quotient readout survived. It does not mean generator
alias.

`mirror preserved` means the named mirror row survived for its scope. It does
not identify untouched frames.

## Exclusion Language

- Exclude generator identity when only basin or fixed point matches.
- Exclude physical-semigroup claims when GKSL/generator validity is not shown.
- Exclude global mirror claims from pairwise mirror evidence.
- Keep promotion blocked: this distillate is a card input, not an admitted S5
  result.
