# Foundation Research Digest

## State representations at L0
- 9/9 survive
- purification ranks highest
- coherence vector is the best d=4-extensible representation
- eigenvalues are blind to phase/noncommutation
- Husimi and Wigner are lossy/smoothed

## Geometry at L0
- trace distance, Bures, Fubini-Study, QFI, and QGT survive
- Hilbert-Schmidt as sole geometry is too flat
- relative entropy is not a metric
- real-only restrictions kill Berry/curvature
- commutative operators kill nontrivial geometry

## Entropy / information
- spectral families line up on qubit mixedness ordering
- diagonal Shannon is basis dependent
- product-proxy conditional entropy adds no signal in the current L/R engine
- coherent information and cut-state structure are more informative than total correlation alone

## Bridge / Axis-0 evidence
- Xi_shell is the strongest candidate bridge family in the readout packet
- local-only L|R fails to produce useful quantum advantage
- compression-from-future framing is partially supported, not universal

## Core primitives suggested by the research
- density matrices
- probes
- operator algebra
- partial traces / channels
- admissible geometry on the same carrier
