## What the wiki should absorb

### 1. MSS / least-admissible rung discipline is the center, not a slogan

The useful repo idea is that minimality is not a third root axiom. It is an admission discipline: keep the weakest evolvable survivor that passes the active constraints, and keep plural survivor branches live when no exclusion has killed them.

Wiki receiver:

- strengthen `MSS` as a gate, not ontology;
- keep `Reading A/B` live where density/complex carrier status is not resolved;
- treat rung climb as weak-to-strong only after a failure receipt shows the weaker layer loses a needed distinction.

Do **not** rewrite this as “the repo proved the minimal structure.” The observed support is draft doctrine plus scratch diagnostics.

### 2. Probe quotient floor is real but mostly definitional

`probe_quotient_fingerprint_floor_v1` gives a clean floor: `Q = X/~_P` is well-defined for the supplied finite probe table. The checks show full `P` rejects a bad coarsening while erased `P'` admits it across z3/cvc5/Julia Z3.

Honest wiki claim:

```text
probe-relative quotient is the safe base floor for this table;
the solver checks protect consistency/coarsening, not deep carrier discovery.
```

Route this under the `S/~_P` / base-floor wiki language. Do not cite it as density, Hilbert, spinor, or manifold admission.

### 3. Probe-as-carrier inversion is useful as a noncommutation discriminator

`probe_as_carrier_inversion_v0` shows a sharp flip:

- single-generator control: one class, zero distinctions;
- all-commuting control: one class, zero distinctions;
- noncommuting alphabet: six classes, five distinctions.

Honest wiki claim:

```text
order distinctions disappear under commuting controls and survive under the named noncommuting alphabet.
```

This is good support for treating probes/updates as load-bearing. It still does not license a separate support entity, carrier admission, or layer promotion.

### 4. Forced-vs-installed is now a real test family, but one sim did not answer it

There are two separate lessons that must not be collapsed.

- `forced_or_installed_carrier_comparison_v0` is a useful coordinate-uniqueness fixture inside a declared density-matrix contract. It distinguishes incomplete probe readouts from complete `(Z,X,Y)` readouts for that contract.
- `finite_distinguishability_quotient_forced_or_installed_carrier_v0` did **not** earn its headline; the candidate carriers were metadata labels, the strength order was hand-ranked, and the setup could not falsify the installed verdict.

Honest wiki claim:

```text
A real redo must construct bare quotient / simplex / real-operator / complex-density / GPT-cone candidates as math objects and derive the strength preorder structurally.
```

This should feed future base-sim planning, not settle the base.

### 5. Carrier-type matrix and contextuality witnesses sharpen “nonclassical” without overnaming `ρ`

```text
it does not exclude every refined/context-indexed quotient model.
```

This is high-value wiki content because it prevents two opposite mistakes: classical noncontextual collapse and overclaiming `ρ` as uniquely required.
