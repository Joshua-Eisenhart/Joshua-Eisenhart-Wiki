## Deeper Ratchet Concepts Extracted

- The ratchet is a narrowing process over candidates, not a story about a
  preselected final engine. A good result records survivors, killed
  alternatives, controls, and the next constrained layer.
- Big messy source docs belong in a mining/rosetta lane. They may produce
  `EXPORT_CANDIDATE`-style kernels, but the labels and metaphors do not enter
  the guide as authority.
- Pre-Axis machinery is a first-class build target: carrier, geometry,
  transport, differential/chirality/flux candidates, negatives, placement, and
  pre-entropy have to exist before honest Axis entry.
- A geometry stack becomes a ratchet candidate only when composition order is
  load-bearing: `A o B != B o A` on some finite witness, or reversed order
  becomes inconsistent under a proof guard.
- Candidate ratchet order `0 -> 6 -> 5 -> 3 -> 4 -> 1x2` appears in legacy
  material as a roadmap. It is not current proof and should not override lower
  evidence.
- Branch-parallel foundation work remains important: structures recurring
  across feed orders are more meaningful than artifacts of one narrative order.
- Viability-kernel framing remains a live alternative to attractor-basin
  framing. The guide can mention both only with tests and evidence labels.
