## Geometry And Entropy Research

The July 9 bounded repair separates two carriers that earlier prose collapsed:

- the reduced Schmidt state carries radius, entropy, and the BKM/Umegaki metric;
- the global pure-state phase family carries Berry connection and holonomy.

Exact finite results:

- `r=cos(2 eta)` decreases on `0<eta<pi/4`;
- entropy increases on that domain;
- the Umegaki Hessian and spectral BKM metric both give `g_eta_eta=4`;
- depolarization contracts the metric and a unitary preserves it;
- a nonpositive amplifier breaks the positivity/contractivity control;
- the marginal erases phase while the global state retains it;
- the declared Berry rectangle has `-pi*delta_r`, not the older asserted `+2*pi*delta_r`.

Therefore the older nested-shell flux normalization is rejected. A Berry connection exists on the bounded two-parameter family; a nested-torus manifold spine does not follow.
