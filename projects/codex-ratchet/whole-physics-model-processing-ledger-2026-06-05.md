## What Has Been Learned So Far

1. The project is not "derive physics from slogans." It is a
   constraint-admissibility research program.

2. The roots are chosen constraints. Downstream structures are candidates,
   pressured consequences, survivors, or open gaps.

3. The ratchet itself is central. It is the method-object: finite, ordered,
   graveyard-producing, anti-teleological, and dependency-building.

4. Wide exploration and strict admission are not opposites. They are paired:
   explore widely at low claim ceiling; promote narrowly at high claim ceiling.

5. Density matrices make sense as probe-equivalence objects, not as arbitrary
   quantum decoration.

6. Density matrices are the first stable QIT state/readout layer, but rho-only
   engines cannot see the lifted spinor/quaternion path surplus. The lifted
   layer has to keep psi/q/path/interference data when 720-degree or Hopf-fiber
   structure matters.

7. Spinors use quaternions: `C^2`, `H`, `SU(2)`, `Sp(1)`, and `Cl^+(3,0)` are
   overlapping descriptions of the same carrier/algebra family in this lane,
   not clean competitors.

8. Spinor/quaternion, Clifford structure, SU(2)/S3, Hopf, nested tori, and
   chirality are strongly pressured by the source story, but exclusivity is
   still open.

9. Bloch spheres are probably quotient/readout objects, not safe foundations,
   because they can hide lifted phase/path/spinor information.

10. Axis 0 remains the biggest bridge gap. Entropy, cut states, Xi families, and
   FEP prediction error must not be collapsed. The deeper physics-model path is
   to build a finite growable `JK fuzz` entanglement-information field on nested
   geometry, then map it through `Xi_fuzz` into a cut state before reading
   gravity/Axis0 quantities.

11. Axes 3, 4, 5, and 6 have stronger math seats than Axis 0, but the axes are
   still exploratory surfaces, not finished ontology.

12. Terrain/operator math is the most concrete engine layer right now, but its
    counts must be typed: 16 ordered tokens are not 16 terrain placements, and
    neither is automatically the 64-schedule atlas.

13. The corrected token identity is `A1 x A2 x A5 x A6 -> 16 ordered tokens`.
    `A3 x A4 x A5 x A6` gives 8 paired loop-placement signatures, not all 16
    token identities.

14. Terrain-only runtime evidence stays quarantined for operator-axis
    promotion. It can be useful terrain evidence without proving the full
    operator/axis manifold is running.

15. IGT is not payoff physics yet. It is a strategy grammar to attach after the
    engine mechanics have explicit readouts.

16. Carnot/Szilard are reference baselines, not identity claims.

17. Holodeck/perception is a real downstream application lane: prediction-first
    world modeling, context-coupled memory, and reconstruction. It is not the
    proof of the physics model.
