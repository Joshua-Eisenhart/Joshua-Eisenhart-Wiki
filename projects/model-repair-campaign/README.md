# Open External Model Repair Registry and Seed Campaigns v0.2

Status: working research program; no scientific admission.

Claim ceiling: open discovery registry, source-informed repair hypotheses and executable campaign contracts. A green campaign validates the wrapper and its controls. It does not establish Joshua's framework, any repaired source model, or a unification claim as canon.

Process authority: [`projects/codex-ratchet/RATCHET_SPEC.md`](../codex-ratchet/RATCHET_SPEC.md) v0.5. The only root is constrained distinguishability. Objects, partitions, probabilities, carriers, causal arrows, assembly grammars, agents, blankets, geometries and gate orders remain candidates until earned within a finite packet.

## Scope correction: four names are seeds, not the field

Friston, Levin, Cronin and Wolfram were the first examples Joshua named. They are not a closed contender list, the four best models, or the boundary of the repair program.

The current channel ledger already contains 354 official RSS episodes, 176 broad title candidates and 57 initial program rows. Only 143 episodes were mapped; 75 remained explicitly untriaged. A further 136 were provisionally marked `context / likely exclude`, and that filter incorrectly buried several useful programs. The GitHub graveyard is also incomplete: it durably preserves 44 scratch rows while reporting 86 cumulative diagnostics.

The correct architecture is:

```text
open candidate registry
→ source-fidelity dossiers
→ role-specific Pareto frontiers
→ selected source-native repair campaigns
→ experiments, controls and receipts
```

Candidate intake remains open to channel guests, cited researchers, executable repositories, datasets, critics, mathematical tools and newly discovered programs. Familiarity, fame and episode count are not ranking evidence.

The four detailed adapters below are seed campaigns because they were named first and expose different repair surfaces. New candidates can precede them when their source fidelity, claim coverage, kill power and acquisition cost place them on the active research-scheduling frontier.

## Central proposal

Joshua's strongest potential contribution to an external model is usually not a replacement equation. It is an outer discovery-and-admission process that forces the model to expose its installed ontology, generate rivals outside that ontology, collapse behavioral aliases, execute schedule and boundary alternatives, retain plural weakest-survivor frontiers, and face interventions that can kill the transplant.

Each source model remains source-native:

1. reproduce the source result before modifying it;
2. record every installed assumption;
3. compile a finite behavior surface under explicit probes and budgets;
4. generate source-native rivals and Joshua-transplanted rivals at high diversity;
5. collapse parameterizations that are behaviorally indistinguishable;
6. execute rival demand groupings, gate orders and intervention schedules;
7. admit only packet-relative weakest survivors under several declared weakness hypotheses;
8. retain immutable evidence receipts while allowing conclusions to be demoted, split or killed;
9. require a novel discriminating prediction before claiming that a model was improved.

This is a model-repair tournament, not a vocabulary merger.

## Registry roles and rankings

Rank source-native program cards, not people. One person may contribute several separable programs, and one program may occupy several roles:

- **competitor** — makes an overlapping foundational, bridge or domain claim;
- **donor** — supplies a mechanism, theorem, representation or algorithm;
- **empirical testbed** — supplies interventions, observations, datasets or apparatus;
- **mathematical tool** — supplies exact inference, quotienting, search or verification machinery;
- **critic/falsifier** — supplies a no-go theorem, counterexample or assumption audit.

There is no permanent weighted `best model` score. Rank separate packet-relative frontiers for foundational depth, formal precision, empirical contact, executable maturity, donor value, critic strength, repair leverage and acquisition cost. If one program has the weaker ontology while another has stronger recovered physics, they remain incomparable until a discriminating packet resolves the relevant claim.

The first major frontier missing from the four seeds concerns Joshua's own operation of seeing highly correlated components as one thing. Correlation alone cannot license objecthood because a common driver can generate false unity. Test at least four rival object compilers:

1. Crutchfield/Shalizi minimal predictive causal states;
2. Krakauer–Bertschinger–Olbrich–Ay–Flack information-theoretic individuals;
3. Tishby–Pereira–Bialek information-bottleneck compression relative to declared relevance;
4. Hoel intervention-relative causal emergence.

Strip their primitive probability, time and causality commitments, then compare predictive sufficiency, intervention closure, persistence, relabeling stability and minimality. This family may bind earlier than any of the four seed campaigns.

Other immediate restored channel cards include John Donoghue, Simon Saunders, David Wallace, Ivette Fuentes, Eric Weinstein, Urs Schreiber, Tim Maudlin, Max Tegmark, Graham Priest, Scott Aaronson and David Sloan Wilson. Curt Jaimungal's own theory also receives a program card rather than being treated as channel context.

Machine-readable intake and lifecycle fields live in [`candidate_registry_v0_2.json`](candidate_registry_v0_2.json).

## 1. Friston adapter: FEP inside the Ratchet

### Preserve

- variational free energy as an inference objective inside a declared generative model;
- expected free energy and sophisticated inference, including epistemic exploration;
- model evidence/reduction, hierarchical models and explicit viability work;
- distinctions among statistical, dynamical and variational blanket claims.

Joshua should not claim that active inference lacks exploration. The addition is exploration outside the current generative-model language, followed by an independently enforced admission process.

### Reopen

Represent a candidate as

```text
m = (carrier, partition, dynamics, instruments, recognition family,
     generative family, update law, objective/divergence, viability constraints)
```

Generate rivals over classical Markov kernels, higher-order history models, CPTP dynamics and process-memory candidates; proposed partitions including no blanket; multiple prior/preference families; and KL, Umegaki, Bures, Rényi, trace-distance and other licensed objectives. None is installed at the root.

The adapter emits finite intervention-conditioned predictions, actions, blanket residuals, flow-alignment residuals, viability outcomes and resource traces. Equivalent parameterizations collapse before survivor counting.

The existing Joshua QIT/FEP simulations remain donor scaffolds only. They currently install such ingredients as a GKSL fixed point, Umegaki divergence, diagonal/classical conditional mutual information, a prior/likelihood family or direct state access. Their honest `scratch_diagnostic` ceiling should be preserved while those ingredients become rival candidates.

### First tournament

Use four blinded worlds:

1. a linear stochastic system satisfying the declared blanket and flow assumptions;
2. the same system with direct internal-external coupling;
3. a statistical blanket whose history/solenoidal conditions break the inferential-flow map;
4. a noncommuting instrument sequence with memory and order effects.

Test every small partition, including no blanket. Hold out interventions, not only observations. Explicitly test the gap between `E[f(X)]` and `f(E[X])`. For quantum candidates, use full quantum conditional mutual information plus a recovery-channel test; order sensitivity alone does not earn quantum structure.

### Kill the transplant if

- it falsely finds a blanket or inferential map in worlds 2 or 3;
- relabeling, relisting, rechunking or equivalent parameterization changes the winner;
- a classical history model is as weak and predictive as the quantum lane in world 4;
- the correctly specified FEP baseline predicts interventions equally well with less machinery;
- success on synthetic worlds produces no later discriminating biological prediction.

Primary starting points: [particular physics](https://arxiv.org/abs/1906.10184), [2023 FEP synthesis](https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.06387), [sophisticated inference](https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.04120), [blanket critique](https://arxiv.org/abs/2001.06408), [dynamical restrictions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2105.11203), and [particular flows response](https://arxiv.org/abs/2205.09595).

## 2. Levin adapter: discover the agent and the target

### Preserve

- real perturbation and rescue experiments in morphogenesis;
- bioelectric networks as control surfaces rather than merely correlates;
- multiscale competency and plastic anatomical goal-seeking;
- the useful idea that cognitive scale concerns the spatiotemporal reach of goals and actions.

### Reopen

Do not predeclare the organism, module, target morphology or cognitive light cone. Candidate cards propose:

```text
(boundary, observable alphabet, perturbation set, target set,
 transition/control law, memory depth, budget, viability demand)
```

For each candidate, estimate the finite set of states whose distinctions can be reached, maintained or repaired under the declared intervention and budget. Treat that reachability/controllability surface as a testable cognitive-light-cone candidate, not an agency score.

Collapse boundaries and controllers that are indistinguishable under the active intervention packet. Retain a plural MSS antichain when different minimal boundaries or controllers carry different demanded distinctions. Compare source-native attractor control, reaction-diffusion, active inference, reinforcement learning and Joshua-transplanted candidates under the same data and intervention budget.

The first code audit should pin [`LPioL/scalefreecognition`](https://github.com/LPioL/scalefreecognition) at commit `bced77dd90e6a5c67bf7a557610b19ee995db729`. Its French-flag target is supplied; cell inputs include error relative to an ideal stripe size; patch reward/stress use stored target information; and the generated random initialization is overwritten by a fixed reverse flag. Reproduce that source-native result, then run a leakage ablation that removes `cell.goal`, ideal-size error and target-dependent ontogenetic reward before making any claim of spontaneously emerging collective goals.

### First tournament

Choose one public, source-native planarian or bioelectric morphogenesis dataset with interventions and outcomes. Train only on a subset of perturbations. Candidate models must predict:

- which perturbations leave the target basin unchanged;
- which move the system into a different stable anatomical basin;
- which timed or ordered intervention rescues the original target;
- whether the inferred control boundary expands or contracts when probe and energy budgets change.

Run timing reversal, intervention-order permutation, spatial relabeling, sham perturbation, matched-damage and alternative-boundary controls. A descriptive fit to final anatomy is insufficient; the target and boundary must support held-out intervention predictions.

The strongest empirical anchor is serial planarian regeneration after transient gap-junction perturbation, where later amputations reveal a persistent hidden regenerative state and further voltage intervention can restore wild-type outcomes. This is a better test surface for target memory than the French-flag simulation: [primary study](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC5443973/).

### Kill the transplant if

- target basins or agent boundaries are selected using held-out outcome labels;
- the inferred cognitive light cone is only a redescribed correlation radius;
- equally predictive smaller source-native controllers dominate the transplanted frontier;
- boundary/target results fail relabeling, timing or budget controls;
- no new intervention or rescue prediction survives experimental testing.

## 3. Cronin adapter: from one assembly number to a constrained pathway frontier

### Preserve

- the central historical claim: some objects bear evidence of constrained reuse and selection;
- assembly paths and copy number as potentially useful experimental observables;
- exact minimum-path calculations where the declared grammar makes them possible;
- mass-spectrometric and molecular datasets as the empirical surface.

### Reopen

Assembly index is conditional on an object representation, primitive parts, join operations, reuse rules and measurement process. Make that dependency explicit. A candidate becomes

```text
(representation, primitive set, join grammar, pathway ensemble,
 reuse policy, copy evidence, resource/kinetic model, observation map)
```

Enumerate rival grammars and decompositions. Separate high-diversity pathway generation from exact low-temperature admission. Keep all nondominated pathways under step count, resource cost, kinetic plausibility, robustness and copy evidence instead of forcing a single scalar too early. Collapse representations that generate the same finite measurement behavior.

Minimum assembly length is not automatically the historical path. The transplant must quantify when the scalar is stable across admissible representations and when path multiplicity or environmental feasibility changes the conclusion.

### First tournament

Build an exact small-instance corpus where exhaustive dynamic programming, ILP or SMT establishes the full pathway set, then test approximate search on larger molecular graphs. Include:

- the same object under alternative valid fragmentation/encoding schemes;
- different objects with matched assembly index but different pathway multiplicity;
- matched mass/composition with different provenance;
- abiotic high-index and biological low-index controls;
- copy-number perturbations and measurement-noise controls.

The prediction target is held-out provenance or formation constraints, not the assembly score used to construct the features.

Pin Cronin's public reference notebooks in [`croningp/assemblyphysics`](https://github.com/croningp/assemblyphysics) at commit `1235d6f8c7ae756831ace180c70067a019a0ae2b`; preserve their outputs as source fixtures and port rather than silently reinterpret them. For heuristic large cases, report a certified interval `a_lower <= a <= a_upper`, not an exact assembly index. Split “selection” into separate gates for generative bias, reuse, templating/autocatalysis, heredity with variation, differential persistence/reproduction and adaptive evolution.

### Kill the transplant if

- the winner depends materially on an arbitrary encoding or primitive set;
- path count, kinetics and resources add no predictive value beyond assembly index plus copy number;
- labels leak into grammar selection or candidate generation;
- exact small cases disagree with the approximate engine;
- the original Assembly Theory baseline is equally predictive and strictly weaker.

## 4. Wolfram adapter: dependency before causality

### Preserve

- multiway nondeterministic rewriting rather than a single classical history;
- structural state canonicalization, event structure, branchial analysis and observer-dependent quotients;
- existing quotient-reconstruction, multiplicity-propagation and bounded-observer probes;
- causal invariance as one important confluence hypothesis.

The current engine is not simply Newtonian or deterministic. Its classical residue is more precise: symbolic set/hypergraph rewriting, monotone event IDs, forward producer-consumer orientation, nonnegative path multiplicities and a default promotion of dependency to causality.

### Reopen

1. Preserve the current graph but expose it first as a resource-dependency graph. A directed causal interpretation is a later candidate.
2. Treat causal invariance/confluence as one outcome. Schedule divergence is live N01 evidence, not an automatic failure.
3. Add demand-relative behavioral canonicalization alongside structural isomorphism. Several incomparable observer quotients may survive.
4. Replace one canonical observer budget with packet-relative probe, demand and budget populations.
5. Add rival history-weight traits: nonnegative multiplicity, signed real, complex amplitude, matrix/operator-valued and explicitly bracketed nonassociative candidates. These are compiler families, not root commitments.
6. Earn a causal arrow only when interventions on event/rule availability create a relabeling-robust asymmetric effect that survives schedule, observer and reverse-rule controls.

### First tournament

Run identical rules and initial states through:

- the current structural canonicalization plus causal graph;
- a demand-relative behavioral quotient lane;
- an order-open nonconfluent lane;
- classical multiplicity, complex-amplitude and operator-weighted history lanes.

Measure raw states, structural classes, behavioral classes, alias collapse, schedule divergence, endpoint frontiers, interference/cancellation, observable reconstruction, and dimension/curvature stability. Ablate direction labels, causal invariance, phase, product order, bracket order, observer budget and demand families.

### Kill the transplant if

- renaming dependency as noncausal changes no prediction or intervention result;
- behavioral quotients merely duplicate structural isomorphism;
- signed/complex/operator weights add machinery without a discriminating reconstruction or prediction gain;
- schedule divergence cannot be tied to a demanded distinction;
- the current engine is equally predictive, reconstructive and compressive with less structure.

Code audit starting points: [Hypergraph Rewriting Engine](https://github.com/WolframInstitute/HypergraphRewritingEngine), [canonicalization reference](https://github.com/WolframInstitute/HypergraphRewritingEngine/blob/main/reference/CANONICALIZATION.md), [causal graph implementation](https://github.com/WolframInstitute/HypergraphRewritingEngine/blob/main/hypergraph/include/hypergraph/causal_graph.hpp), and the primary [causal-invariance paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2004.14810).

## Tool boundary: repair Lev before letting it adjudicate science

Lev can orchestrate these campaigns, persist lineage and replay receipts, but it is not the scientific authority. Its current constraint/admission flows need rebinding before use:

- `ROOT` must be constrained distinguishability; F01 and N01 are active pressures/hypotheses, not immutable domain axioms;
- a workflow DAG records execution dependency, not ontological causality;
- scientific candidates may contain cycles when execution remains finitely bounded;
- gate boundaries and orders are proposal populations, not a fixed forward ladder;
- receipts are append-only, while conclusions remain defeasible and demotable;
- preinstalled Hilbert/QIT vocabulary must be removed from the root contract;
- near-duplicate detection must use finite behavioral equivalence, not name or Jaccard similarity alone;
- every campaign executes schedule populations and reports packet-relative plural MSS frontiers.

After this repair, use FlowMind to declare campaign packets and controls, Orchestration to run source-native adapters, Graph to store evidence/lineage, and the Event Bus to audit execution events. Do not treat the Event Bus's “causality plane” label as a scientific causal claim.

## Seed implementation order

Two rankings matter:

| Rank | Engineering tractability | Scientific payoff |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Cronin: exact finite pathway cases make the fastest honest adapter | Levin: real interventions can force or kill the transplant |
| 2 | Wolfram: public finite engine and explicit canonicalization hooks | Friston: the strongest meta-model-selection comparison |
| 3 | Friston: controlled synthetic worlds before biological claims | Wolfram: the sharpest test of dependency, order and classical residue |
| 4 | Levin: data harmonization and experimental validation are expensive | Cronin: valuable if it gains robustness beyond a grammar-relative scalar |

This orders only the first four implementation examples, not the open registry. Recommended seed sequence:

1. implement the exact Assembly pathway packet to validate adapter mechanics;
2. port behavioral quotient, schedule and weight hooks into a Wolfram-side experimental branch;
3. run the blinded FEP assumption tournament;
4. spend the resulting mature harness on a Levin intervention dataset;
5. recompute the open research-scheduling frontier after every source reproduction;
6. extract a common interface only after at least two source-native adapters work.

The last constraint matters. A broad workbench built first would freeze unearned assumptions into an API.

## Promotion ladder

Every result carries one of these ceilings:

```text
source_reproduced
adapter_runs
controls_flip
held_out_prediction
cross_domain_semiconjugacy
constraint_necessity_within_packet
```

Analogy or shared vocabulary is below this ladder. “Joshua fixes X” is licensed only after the source baseline is reproduced, the transplant wins on a discriminating held-out test under equal budgets, and an ablation removes that advantage. Even then the claim is packet-relative and defeasible.
