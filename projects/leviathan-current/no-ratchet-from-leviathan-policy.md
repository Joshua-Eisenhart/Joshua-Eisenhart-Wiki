# No Ratchet From Leviathan Policy

## Policy

Codex Ratchet should never be described as deriving from Leviathan OS.

## Rationale

The owner correction for this pass states that Leviathan is not a source for Codex Ratchet. The wiki should treat convergence as Josh/Ratchet-origin unless future direct evidence proves a narrow, specific exception.

## Allowed formulations

- “Leviathan contains Josh/Ratchet-aligned concepts.”
- “FlowMind hosts or expresses a Josh-root constraint frame.”
- “Lev docs explicitly credit Josh with identifying constraint-system unification.”
- “Leviathan and Codex Ratchet converge around constraint vocabulary, but convergence is not origin.”

## Forbidden formulations

- “Leviathan is the origin source for Ratchet.”
- “Leviathan originated the Ratchet model.”
- “JP originated the Ratchet root constraints.”
- “Codex Ratchet imports its constraint doctrine from Lev.”
- “Leviathan proves or subsumes Codex Ratchet.”

## Enforcement

Any page that discusses Josh, Ratchet, FlowMind, Wizard, or Leviathan constraints must preserve the no-import direction.
