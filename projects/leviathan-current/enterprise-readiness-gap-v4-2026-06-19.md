# Leviathan Enterprise Readiness Gap

Enterprise readiness is a separate product track. It must not be inferred from architecture.

## Checklist

- ABAC and leases;
- audit trails;
- kill switches;
- cost tracking;
- resource governor;
- approval queues;
- compliance posture;
- admin surfaces;
- secrets handling;
- deployment topology;
- observability;
- incident response;
- update/migration story;
- data retention.

## Safe verdict

Current status remains not enterprise-ready unless proof pages establish the gates above at a current SHA.
