# Leviathan Vision Strategic Truth vs Magnus Opus Overhang

`docs/VISION.md` contains strategic truth and speculative overhang. The wiki should preserve both without merging them.

## Stable strategic ideas

- contract-first substrate;
- Poly protocol vs Poly runtime;
- authoring -> IR -> derived registry -> consumers;
- op-owned guidance;
- thin terminal / rich GenUI;
- receipt-first economics;
- predictive kernel half;
- local-first / federate-ready;
- representation boundary;
- FlowMind as experiment substrate;
- not merging Hermes or an external agent into core.

## High-risk overhang

- living mesh;
- thousands of installs thinking in unison;
- DAO/tax/economic claims;
- avatar / mirror / human-collective experiment language;
- "magnus opus" claims.

## Safe posture

VISION is the strategic-destination layer. Its mesh/economic/avatar sections remain long-horizon vision unless backed by current runtime/product proof.
