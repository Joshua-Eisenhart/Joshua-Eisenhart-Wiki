# Packet 11 Deeper Nuance Attribution Pack Intake

## Intake Verdict

The deeper nuance and attribution pack is useful as a second-pass addendum. It sharpens provenance, delusion/overclaim pressure, and the Josh/JP boundary. It does not prove runtime health and does not edit `lev-os/leviathan`.

Audit result: **pass with cautions**.

## Owner-Correction Accepted

The pack preserves the current owner correction:

```text
Josh = Joshua Eisenhart = Codex Ratchet maker.
Leviathan OS is not a source for Codex Ratchet.
If Codex Ratchet and Leviathan converge, the convergent Ratchet-side material should be treated as Josh-origin unless direct contrary evidence exists.
```
