## 1. The Three-Tier Development Mirrors & Native Currencies

The system structures recursive, bottom-up accountability and P2P development through three separate, parallel mirrors, each utilizing its own specific currency to manage transactions:

```text
  ┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
  │              ECOSYSTEM DEVELOPMENT MIRROR              │
  │  - Engine: Community Reflection Engine (Ecosystem)     │

  │  - Metrics: Vitality, Innovation, Cohesion             │
  └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### 1.1 Service Barter Currency (SBC) — Individual
*   **Role:** Cryptocurrency enabling service-based barter exchanges (trading labor/skills without traditional money).
*   **Minting:** Newly minted by advancing Mirror ranks in the training system. Most SBC is earned by teaching certified services to peers, with minimal SBC earned for individual training.
*   **Impact:** High SBC earnings from useful/rare skills raise Status Attractiveness.

### 1.2 Cooperative Resource Currency (CRC) — Organizational
*   **Role:** Cryptocurrency enabling goods-based barter exchanges (trading physical assets, tools, or property).
*   **Minting:** Earned for sharing physical assets loaded into the organizational registry (e.g., using personal homes or land as business infrastructure) and for inter-company OpenFinance collaboration.
*   **Impact:** Tracks group contributions and cooperative asset pooling.

### 1.3 Ecosystem Contribution Currency (ECC) — Ecosystem
*   **Role:** Cryptocurrency enabling barter of shared ecosystem assets (knowledge, equity/shares in cooperative businesses, cultural contributions, and HOA rights).
*   **Minting:** Minted via ecosystem training rank advancement and shared regional asset pooling.
*   **Impact:** Rewards companies for supporting employee mobility and OpenHR.

## 3. Decentralized OpenHR & OpenFinance

The Mirrors coordinate resource and career mobility without centralized management:

*   **OpenHR (Ecosystem-Wide Mobility):** Career development is managed at the ecosystem level, completely independent of specific jobs.
    *   *Employee Poaching:* Companies are incentivized to poach and compete. 
    *   *Attractiveness Rewards:* Companies that maintain high "Company Attractiveness" are rewarded via ECC, encouraging desirable, high-development workplaces.
*   **OpenFinance (Resource Management):**
    *   *Asset Loading:* Individuals load physical assets (homes, tools, possessions) into the registry.
    *   *Selective Sharing:* Allows individuals to share assets as business infrastructure (e.g., using land or spare rooms for cooperatives) while maintaining strict control over sharing parameters to preserve privacy.
    *   *OpenFinance Collaboration:* Peer companies expose business problems and financial blocks to one another on public ledgers for collaborative problem-solving.
