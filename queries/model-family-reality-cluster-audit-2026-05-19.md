## Redundancy and collapse risks

### Risk 1 — one giant ToE page

The model of reality should not be one giant synthesis page. That would hide the source differences and make the wiki harder to learn. The safe route is a reading path plus packet ledgers.

### Risk 2 — self-similarity becoming sameness

The five-framework doctrine says Holodeck, QIT, science method, IGT, and Leviathan share a generator. It does not say they are the same object. Preserve the separate pages and show the shared generator through crosswalks.

### Risk 3 — generated source over-promotion

Packet B and related Grok/Gemini PDFs contain useful summaries, but they also include hallucination repairs, confidence language, and generated outside-reference claims. They should feed extraction ledgers, not authority pages.

### Risk 4 — legacy book hazard leakage

Dark Empress contains valuable cosmology/cybernetics/governance source material and also dominion/collapse/authoritarian pressure. Its page should remain source-history with hazard fences.

### Risk 5 — result-router proof leakage

QIT/geometry/result pages can support carriers and evidence ceilings. They do not automatically prove cosmology, psychology, Leviathan runtime, or Holodeck implementation.
