## 1. The Three Proposed Axioms (Chapter 12)

The core physics framework of *The Dark Empress* is crystallized in three formal axioms that describe how space-time and universes bootstrap, stabilize, and select themselves through probability:

### Axiom 1: Evolution of Space-Time (Sequential Multiverse)
*   **Motif:** "Universes could be born out of other universes."
*   **Mechanism:** The multiverse is separated by lengths in time (cycles) rather than merely by spatial distance. The end state / boundary conditions of a preceding universe affect the starting conditions of the subsequent universe.
*   **Result:** Small improvements or stable structures can transfer across cycles. This creates a ratchet-like evolution of physical parameters across universe-generations, accumulating complexity.

### Axiom 2: The Consistency of Time (Survival through Persistency)
*   **Motif:** Only what is internally consistent can persist through time.
*   **Mechanism:** "The most likely thing that could happen, is that the universe had its very first stable state. It didn't just come and go. It endured a bit."
*   **Result:** The vacuum (the simplest possible state) is the most probable first state because it requires the fewest prior conditions and has the lowest impedance/tension.

### Axiom 3: The Probability of the Possible and Impossible (Selection)
*   **Motif:** Impossible structures are excluded not by external decree but by their inability to survive their own internal contradictions.
*   **Mechanism:** Reality bootstraps from pure random probability. As probability structures evolve, systems find least-tension paths (eigenvectors) that endure.
*   **Result:** Survival through time is the primary cosmological selection criterion (viability).

## 2. Event Horizon Coordinate Inversion (Chapter 11 & 13)

The book describes a radical coordinate shift that occurs when crossing a black hole event horizon, transforming the boundary of a collapsing singularity into the coordinate center of a newborn universe:

### The Crossing Inversion
*   **From the Outside:** An observer falling toward a black hole sees space and time dilate. The event horizon is a boundary in space-time where the speed of causality (the speed of light) is reached.
*   **From the Inside:** Once crossing the event horizon, the coordinate geometry flips. The event horizon is no longer a boundary in the distance—it behaves like pure empty space and becomes the **origin/center** of a massive, expanding spherical space-time.
*   **The Singularity Surface:** The singularity of the black hole is no longer a point in front of the observer; it is projected and spread out as a spherical surface surrounding them in every direction (e.g., "if the singularity was red, you would see red in every direction at a singular distance").
*   **Emergent White Hole:** Matter and energy falling into the black hole from the parent universe enter the interior universe by flinging out from the central origin point (the coordinate center/center of the sphere), appearing to the interior observer as a white-hole source flinging matter in all directions from nothingness (Big Bang).
