# Factory Handoff Skill v4.2

authority_status: canonical-skill

Find bottlenecks, queues, handoffs, leverage points, and operational next moves.

## When Used

Use as a Factory child under Decision, Failure, Follow-Up, or management when a route needs operational reality:

- what is blocked;
- what can be handed off;
- what is slow or brittle;
- what one move creates the most leverage.

## Method

1. Name the current work unit in one sentence.
2. Identify the active queue or bottleneck.
3. Decide whether the right move is continue, split, reroute, hand off, or stop.
4. Convert that into a concrete next action.
5. Name the stop/block condition.

Do not summarize the whole Wizard run. Stay on the assigned parent route and child role.

## Return Fields

- bottleneck;
- next handoff;
- queue risk;
- leverage move;
- stop/block condition.

Also include the common child receipt fields required by `WIZARD_v4_2.md`: child role id, compact MMM loaded, mini-MMM loaded, distinct delta, evidence boundary, output patch, and status.
