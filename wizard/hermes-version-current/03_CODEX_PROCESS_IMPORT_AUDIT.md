## What Codex is doing well

Codex has made the general Wizard operational by binding it to a concrete repo process:

1. authority order is explicit;
2. full MMM/mini-MMM boot paths are concrete;
3. visible routes require receipts;
4. parent/child/external worker pools stay separate;
5. tools are not counted as child subagents;
6. controller owns final truth labels and shared-state mutation;
7. sim/proof/tool work has a strict micro-first stage gate;
8. follow-ups are supposed to be made, scouted, and audited before being called preworked;
9. stale or missing child obligations must remain visible;
10. worker claims are checked against files/results, not accepted as prose.
