# Root Axiom Card

Core rule: identity is probe-relative rather than primitive. Treat `a=a iff a~b` as the short anchor, then route detailed derivation work to the archived long note at `wiki/_archive/harness_2026-04-17_steward_trim/15_root_axiom_card.md`.

Operational consequences:
- cite probe family `M` when using identity or equivalence language
- prefer admissibility / survival language over primitive ontology claims
- preserve divergence until exclusion structure narrows it
- keep subject/probed split inside the probe-relative quotient, not as a privileged outside stance

Output checks:
- do not state bare identity or equality without probe context
- do not promote a surviving candidate to closure without exclusion evidence
- use `03_language_discipline.md` for rewrite patterns and `17_pre_emit_audit.md` for the compact check loop
