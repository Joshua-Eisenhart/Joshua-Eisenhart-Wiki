# MMM Word Ratchet

Purpose: build a constraint ratchet on words, phraselets, and clause shapes before rewriting mini-MMMs.

Status: active harness language-reservoir scaffold. This page is not a finished lexicon. It is the admission protocol for the lexicon.

## Core claim

A mini-MMM should not mainly be a rule list. It should be a dense language field that makes aligned continuations easier than drift continuations.

The unit under selection is not only a technical keyword. The unit can be:
- a common word
- a phraselet
- a preposition habit
- a negation pattern
- a sentence stem
- a contrast pair
- a cadence class
- a domain metaphor
- a refusal clause
- a status phrase

A single word such as `under`, `held`, `only`, `where`, `same`, or `left` does not prove alignment. A large field of such words, used in the same grammar, can reveal and reinforce the frame.

## Root admission test

A word or phrase enters the reservoir only if it makes the next sentence more likely to preserve:
- finitude
- order / non-commutation
- non-Cartesian framing
- anti-Platonic restraint
- constraint on distinguishability
- correlation before object
- symmetry before identity
- probability before certainty
- live alternatives before winner-story
- earned hierarchy before primitive rank
- evidence status before closure

If the candidate word pulls toward primitive identity, abstract essence, story-causality, completed infinity, flat equality by reflex, or unreceipted authority, it is constrained, quarantined, or excluded.

## Identity contamination audit

The hidden problem is not only the visible equality sign. It is the unearned permission to carry a name, object, type, role, or state across a change without naming the probe under which that carry is admissible.

Audit every research lane for these smuggled assumptions:
- stable object before probe
- same element before equivalence relation
- same type before construction
- identity morphism before operational comparison
- definitional equality before witness
- object permanence before measurement
- person / office / role continuity without practice
- social authority without conduct
- law / essence / structure treated as self-standing

Aligned rewrite:
- `X is X` -> `X remained indistinguishable from the relevant alternative under M`
- `same object` -> `same for this probe family / resolution / role`
- `the structure persists` -> `the tested invariants reappeared under the active transformations`
- `the office has authority` -> `the office carries authority while role conduct and institutional practice keep coupling`

## Ratchet ladder

Positive path:
1. candidate word or phraselet
2. admitted in one local frame
3. admitted across several frames
4. promoted to sentence stem
5. load-bearing in mini-MMM probe
6. canonical reservoir term

Negative path:
1. candidate word or phraselet
2. drift-prone
3. context-limited
4. quarantined
5. excluded from boot surfaces

A word can be admitted in one frame and excluded in another. `hierarchy` is an example: admitted when it names observed nested order, role obligation, dependency, practice, or constraint layering; excluded when it becomes primitive rank or authority immune to conduct.

- the witness returned
- the readout separated

### 6. Anti-collapse / plurality

### 7. Hierarchy / role / ritual / inherited order

candidate words:
hierarchy, order, rank, role, office, rite, ritual, deference, obedience, authority, lineage, inheritance, parent, child, elder, student, teacher, apprentice, practice, conduct, reverence, remonstrance, duty, obligation, transmission, station, cultivated, precedent

admitted phraselets:
- earned hierarchy
- role borne by conduct

### 8. Language / signs / naming

candidate words:

phraselets:
- name follows conduct
- the word carries a constraint

- phraselets, not slogans
- the grammar makes drift harder
- the reservoir is a distribution, not a glossary

## Source-family admission classes

### Authority / internal source
- wiki harness
- hermes-current spine
- project front doors
- repo-current enforcement docs
- working mini-MMM probes and receipts

These define the system language. They outrank external sources.

### Support / adjacent source
- operationalism and measurement theory
- formal verification / proof engineering
- quantum contextuality and indistinguishability
- stochastic thermodynamics and information theory
- viability and attractor basins
- evolutionary epistemology and selection theory
- cybernetics / control / distributed systems
- legal/evidentiary reasoning
- ritual, institution, and role theory

These supply word fields and phrase-shapes, but remain subject to the root admission test.

### Technique reservoir
- Hobbes
- Shakespeare
- Hume
- Montaigne
- James
- Peirce
- Confucian and Daoist classics
- legal opinions and cross-examination
- craft manuals and field notes

These are not target voices. They show how dense language systems shape thought, how role and pressure hold in prose, and how repeated terms form a world.

### Caution / countercorpus
- primitive identity in logic and set theory
- Platonizing structural realism
- category theory when identity morphisms are treated as harmless
- Cartesian subject/object language
- rationalist proof-by-story
- managerial abstraction
- fake consensus language
- causal overreach

These are useful because they show where drift enters.

## Extraction protocol

1. Collect candidate words and phraselets from a source.
2. Record whether the word is common or technical.
3. Record its grammar: what prepositions, verbs, and objects cluster around it.
4. Test the word under root constraints: finitude, non-commutation, distinguishability, anti-primitive identity, anti-Platonism.
5. Classify the candidate as admitted, context-limited, quarantined, or excluded.
6. Write at least one shaped sentence and one drift contrast.
7. Add voice/lane fit only after the word survives the general ratchet.
8. Promote only if it improves a mini-MMM or completion-stem test.

## Drift contrast examples

Drifted:
- The framework proves the correct object.

Shaped:
- The candidate remained in the survivor set under the probes that ran. No discriminator has earned a single object yet.

Drifted:
- The hierarchy is oppressive by nature.

Shaped:
- The hierarchy is real where role, practice, dependency, and conduct keep it coupled. Rank without those receipts is unearned authority, not order.

Drifted:
- Category theory solves primitive identity by focusing on morphisms.

Shaped:
- Category theory weakens object-essentialism in useful ways, but its identity morphisms, equality of arrows, and object persistence still need a probe-relative audit.

Drifted:
- The wiki summarizes the system.

Shaped:
- The wiki is an external language environment. The controller inspects bounded slices, spawns workers over those slices, and accepts only receipts that preserve the live branches.

## Mini-MMM extraction rule

A mini-MMM may pull from this reservoir only after the words and phraselets have survived this page's admission test. The mini-MMM should read like dense aligned language, not like an instruction list.

Good target shape:

> The candidate remains where the probes left it. The name is useful only at the resolution that failed to split it. A stronger object would need a stronger comparison. Until that comparison returns, the branch is held, not closed.

Bad target shape:

> Use nominalist vocabulary. Avoid overclaiming. Preserve branches.

The second is a rule list. The first is a language field.
