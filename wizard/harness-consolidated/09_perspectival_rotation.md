# Perspectival Rotation: One Thing, Many Views

The core methodology: Find maximally correlated things. Rotate the measurement angle until they resolve into one. Not unification by stitching — perspective shift until multiplicity becomes identity.

## Core Pattern

When you find several apparently separate systems that are maximally correlated, ask: What single thing are we measuring from different angles?

**Example domains:**
- Gravity, dark energy, time, space, entropy, entanglement
- Ratchet functionals, Boltzmann brains, stochastic retrocausal selection  
- Bell curves (negative feedback dominated) vs power laws (positive feedback dominated)
- Constraint manifold, FEP prediction-first, Axis 0 entropy gradient

All are the same thing measured along different DOF.

## Worked Example: Gravity/Entropy/Time/Space/Dark-Energy/Entanglement

**View 1 (thermodynamics):** Space expands (entropy increases). Expansion IS time.

**View 2 (gravity):** Objects are pushed apart by dark energy (not pulled by gravity). Expansion is acceleration. Acceleration is force.

**View 3 (information):** Entanglement entropy persists across the expanding space. Information couples sender and receiver despite spatial separation. Gravity keeps them synchronized.

**View 4 (materialism):** Spacetime IS the base material. No empty void. Expansion means more spacetime is created. The rate of creation IS dark energy. The resistance to separation IS gravity.

**The rotation:** Measure the same system from thermodynamic DOF (entropy), relativistic DOF (metric signature), information DOF (entanglement), and material DOF (spacetime density). They describe one phenomenon.

**Implication for workers:** When you encounter divergent narratives (classical vs quantum, geometry vs information, forward vs backward), don't pick one. Ask: What single structure do both perspectives probe? Find the rotation angle where they collapse.

## Why This Matters for Sims

The Rosetta convergence principle: when multiple independent measurement frameworks (QIT, IGT, I-Ching, Jungian, physics engines) recover the same invariant despite different vocabularies, that invariant is load-bearing.

Rosetta labels are earned later, not written into the sim. Sim code and JSON stay pure-math / measured-invariant first; cross-framework naming is a separate analysis pass after independent runs line up.

This is not consensus. This is geometric collapse. They weren't measuring similar things — they were measuring THE SAME THING, and we found the angle.

**LLM failure mode:** The model will smooth contradictions instead of holding divergence. Your job is to keep divergent simulations running in parallel, find where they agree despite differing methods, and report the agreement itself as the signal.

## Application in Harness

- Don't collapse multiple surviving candidates into one story
- Sim all candidates independently
- Report where they agree (that's the signal)
- Let the constraint manifold eliminate what fails later tests
- Treat divergence as structural information, not noise to smooth
