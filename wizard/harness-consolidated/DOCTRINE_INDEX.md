# Doctrine Index — Pull Conditions

Second-layer doctrine pages, indexed by when to open them. The priming layer (`SALIENCE_LOADER.md`) + grammar layer (`03_language_discipline.md`, `19_grammar.md`, `20_phrasebook.md`, `17_pre_emit_audit.md`) covers most work. Open a doctrine page when the priming + grammar layer has not resolved the edge case in front of you.

Router only. Read targets directly.

## Core doctrine (Codex + Lev shared)

- **`~/wiki/concepts/nominalist-cs-framing.md`** — open when translating repo-native terms (lego, candidate, probe, artifact, canonical) into controller/state-machine language; when a reader is importing geometry as primitive; when summary-first drift appears in prose. Carries the render order and the compact translation table.
- **`~/wiki/concepts/llm-bias-inversion-rules.md`** — open when verb discipline alone is not holding. Six inversions: reification → survival, narrative-smoothing → divergence, forward-causation → constraint-selection, universal → context-bound, object-first → relation-first, compression → divergence-preservation.
- **`~/wiki/concepts/controller-prompt-rules.md`** — open for status-vocabulary edges, worker-controller boundary language, and when a candidate is being promoted without exclusion evidence.
- **`~/wiki/concepts/nominalist-translation-rules.md`** — open when a page needs T2→T3 rewrite; carries the 7 translation rules (Object→survivor, Creation→selection, Essence→probe-relative, Causation→coupling, Truth→admissibility, Identity→distinguishability-class, Universal→context-bound).
- **`~/wiki/concepts/nominalism-in-this-system.md`** — open for deeper derivation from `a = a iff a ~ b`. Anti-reification basis.
- **`~/wiki/concepts/anti-reification-and-nominalism-reference.md`** — open when three reification traps appear: bare-object, primitive-causality, primitive-identity. Worked examples.
- **`~/wiki/concepts/formal-methods-and-witness-discipline-reference.md`** — open when witness asymmetry or fail-closed enforcement is load-bearing; UNSAT primacy context.
- **`~/wiki/concepts/qit-vocabulary-discipline-reference.md`** — open for QIT-lane term routing and ontology-smuggling blocks.
- **`~/wiki/concepts/support-first-constraint-manifold-dependency-chain.md`** — open when support/carrier/manifold ordering is at stake; `runs on` as earned sim question.
