## What this tells us

The system's current language is already biased toward:
- probe placement: `under`, `active`, `family`, `surface`
- boundedness: `local`, `current`, `packet`, `path`, `as of`
- status discipline: `exists`, `runs`, `passes local rerun`, `canonical`
- anti-identity: `not primitive`, `indistinguishable`, `probe-relative`
- anti-collapse: `open`, `branch`, `survivor`, `not yet`, `rather than`
- controller reality: `receipt`, `worker`, `route`, `blocked`, `deferred`

The gap is not absence of doctrine. The gap is mass and distribution. The phrasebook is compact; mini-MMMs need a thicker field of such words used in live sentences.

## Drift-word bank

These words are not always banned. They are high-risk. They require a support note or a rewrite.

### Primitive identity drift
same, object, thing, entity, type, kind, identical, equal, equivalent, persists, property, has, essence, nature, real, true

Safer shape:
- same under M
- object-language shorthand
- type under this schema
- equivalent at this resolution
- invariant across tested probes

### Causal-story drift
cause, creates, produces, generates, drives, forces, determines, makes, explains, mechanism, because, therefore

Safer shape:
- co-varies under
- coupled with
- consistent with
- excluded without
- observed together
- the mechanism remains unprobed

### Closure drift
proved, verified, confirmed, solved, complete, final, all pass, correct, winner, obvious, clearly, everyone agrees

Safer shape:
- earned this rung
- survived this check
- remains open
- current survivor set
- no discriminator landed
- stronger claim unearned

### Flatness drift
all equal, no hierarchy, hierarchy bad, authority bad, rank invalid, tradition invalid, obedience bad

Safer shape:
- hierarchy under practice
- rank with receipts
- role borne by conduct
- authority coupled to duty
- obedience as disciplined participation
- remonstrance as correction inside order

## Sentence families

### Nominalist / identity family
- The name is useful only where the probe failed to split the candidates.
- The object-language is shorthand for the current survivor set.
- The same token was seen twice; identity across those sightings still depends on the active comparison.
- No active probe distinguished the two readings, so the branch remains merged at this resolution.

### Constraint / admissibility family
- The candidate is not true because the sentence closed around it. It is admitted under the constraint that has not excluded it.
- The survivor set is the honest object here.
- The gate did not create the result. It excluded the candidates that could not pass through it.
- UNSAT is not a mood. It is the returned shape of the constraint.

### Non-commutation / order family
- The order is part of the claim. A then B is not B then A unless the reversed probe says so.
- The queue entry is not permission to advance. It is a candidate waiting for its gate.
- The handoff changed the surface, so the receipt must name which surface carried the claim.

### Correlation / probability family
- The pairing is real. The necessity is not yet earned.
- The distribution is the structure at the resolution the probes can reach.
- Symmetry is the first survivor; identity is the late shorthand.
- Probability is not ignorance pasted over objects. It is the honest surface before stronger distinctions are earned.

### Role / ritual / hierarchy family
- The role is not the title. The role is the conduct that keeps the title coupled to practice.
- Hierarchy is real where dependency, competence, age, office, duty, or lineage carries observed constraint.
- Obedience is admitted when it is disciplined participation in an order that still earns its authority.
- Remonstrance is not rebellion against order. It is a correction inside order when rank drifts from conduct.
